# Heroes of Horror — Classes (Staging)
**Source:** Heroes of Horror (HoH) Chapter 5, pp. 82–119

These are new base classes and prestige classes from HoH. Once reviewed, move to `srd/classes/`.

## Heroes of Horror Base Classes

### Archivist
- **Source:** HoH p. 82
- **HD:** d6 | **BAB:** Medium (as cleric) | **Saves:** Fort good, Ref poor, Will good
- **Armor:** Light and medium, no shields | **Weapons:** Simple
- **Casting:** Divine, Intelligence-based, prepared from a prayerbook (like a wizard but divine). Starts with all 0-level cleric spells + 3 first-level. Gains 2 new spells/level (must be cleric list); can learn non-cleric divine spells from scrolls. Int 10 + spell level to cast; DC 10 + level + Int mod.
- **Skills (4 + Int/level):** Concentration, Craft, Decipher Script, Diplomacy, Gather Information, Heal, all Knowledge skills, Profession, Search, Spellcraft.
- **Key Features:**
  - **Dark Knowledge** (move action, 3/day scaling to 9/day): Targets aberrations, elementals, magical beasts, outsiders, or undead within 60 ft. Requires Knowledge check DC 15 (appropriate type). Results scale with how much the check exceeds DC (base/+10/+20):
    - *Tactics* (1st): +1/+2/+3 to allies' attacks vs. that creature type.
    - *Puissance* (5th): +1/+2/+3 on saves vs. creature's abilities.
    - *Foe* (8th): +1d6/+2d6/+3d6 bonus damage vs. creature.
    - *Dread Secret* (11th): dazzles/dazes/stuns target creature 1 round.
    - *Foreknowledge* (14th): +1/+2/+3 insight to allies' AC vs. creature.
  - **Scribe Scroll** (bonus feat, 1st)
  - **Lore Mastery** (2nd, 7th, 13th, 17th): +2 Decipher Script + one Knowledge skill per selection
  - **Still Mind** (4th): +2 saves vs. enchantment
  - **Bonus Feat** (10th, 20th): Skill Focus (Knowledge), Spell Focus, metamagic, or item creation feat
- **Role:** Divine scholar/support. Exceptional against specific monster types. Not a frontliner; best in parties with multiple divine casters. Natural gateway to many prestige classes.
- **Taint note:** Designed to study evil without being corrupted by it; the class is taint-resistant by training, not by mechanical immunity.

### Dread Necromancer
- **Source:** HoH p. 84
- **HD:** d6 | **BAB:** Medium (as cleric) | **Saves:** Fort poor, Ref poor, Will good
- **Armor:** Light only; casts dread necromancer spells in light armor without spell failure. Proficient with simple weapons + 1 martial weapon.
- **Casting:** Arcane, Charisma-based, spontaneous (full spell list known automatically by level). DC 10 + level + Cha mod.
- **Skills (2 + Int/level):** Bluff, Concentration, Craft, Decipher Script, Disguise, Hide, Intimidate, Knowledge (arcana), Knowledge (religion), Profession, Spellcraft.
- **Key Features:**
  - **Charnel Touch** (at-will, 1/round, Su): Melee touch, 1d8 + 1/4 levels negative energy (harms living, heals undead). Can deliver via Spectral Hand.
  - **Rebuke Undead** (Su, 1st): Functions as evil cleric.
  - **Lich Body / DR** (2nd): DR 2/bludgeoning and magic, scaling to DR 4 (7th), 6 (11th), 8 (15th).
  - **Negative Energy Burst** (3rd, 1/day scaling to 4/day): 5-ft. burst, 1d4/level negative energy (Will half); heals undead.
  - **Advanced Learning** (4th, 8th, 12th, 16th, 20th): Adds one cleric or wizard necromancy spell of a level already castable.
  - **Mental Bastion** (4th): +2 on saves vs. sleep, stun, paralysis, poison, disease (becomes +4 at 14th).
  - **Fear Aura** (5th, Su): 5-ft. radius, Will DC 10 + ½ level + Cha or shaken.
  - **Scabrous Touch** (6th, Su, 1/day scaling to 3/day): Adds contagion effect to next charnel touch (Fort DC 10 + ½ level + Cha or disease activates immediately).
  - **Summon Familiar** (7th): Can choose imp, quasit, vargouille, or ghostly visage.
  - **Undead Mastery** (8th): Undead created gain +4 Str/Dex and +2 hp/HD; undead control cap = (4 + Cha bonus) HD × level.
  - **Negative Energy Resistance** (9th): +4 on saves vs. negative energy, energy drain, some ability drain.
  - **Light Fortification** (10th): 25% chance to negate critical hits (50% at 17th).
  - **Enervating Touch** (12th, Su): Charnel touch bestows negative levels (up to 2/touch; max ½ level/day; increases to full level/day at 17th).
  - **Craft Wondrous Item** (19th, bonus feat): Prepares for lich phylactery creation.
  - **Lich Transformation** (20th): Becomes a lich (no XP or gold cost for phylactery).
- **Alignment restriction:** Cannot be good.
- **Role:** Combat caster/undead commander with progressive transformation into undeath. Very strong undead control. Pairs well with spectral hand to deliver touch effects safely.

## Heroes of Horror Prestige Classes

### Corrupt Avenger
- **Source:** HoH p. 88
- **Requirements:** Alignment non-evil; BAB +6; Corruption score moderate (per Table 4-1)
- **HD:** d12 | **BAB:** Full | **Saves:** Fort good, Ref poor, Will poor
- **Length:** 10 levels
- **Spellcasting:** Limited arcane spellcasting (Intelligence-based, prepared like wizard); caster level equals Corrupt Avenger level. Requires Corruption score ≥ spell level to cast. Bonus spells based on 10 + Corruption score. Can cast corrupt spells.
- **Key Features:**
  - **Sworn Foe** (1st): Choose one specific monster type or organization. +2 bonus to Bluff/Listen/Sense Motive/Spot/Survival vs. sworn foe (scales to +8 at 10th). When facing sworn foe: rage-like fury (+2 Str/Con) lasting Corruption score rounds; Fort save (DC 15 + 1/round in fury) at end or +1 Corruption.
  - **Taint Suppression** (1st): Physical corruption symptoms can be replaced with Internal Corruption (invisible externally). External symptoms return in tainted areas, disappear 1d4 days after leaving.
  - **Armored Casting** (1st): No arcane spell failure in light armor.
  - **Detect Sworn Foe** (1st, Sp): At will, like detect evil but for sworn foe.
  - **Tainted Strike** (2nd, 1/day → 3/day): Infuse a melee attack with taint for +½ class level damage. No effect on undead or [Evil] subtype creatures.
  - **Grim Resolve** (3rd): Add Cha bonus to all saves (doesn't stack with divine grace).
  - **Frightful Fury** (5th): Entering fury causes all opponents within 30 ft. to make Will save (DC 10 + level + Cha) or become shaken.
  - **Unnerving Fury** (9th): Upgrades to –4 penalty (frightened → unnerved) if Will save failed.
- **Fallen Paladin option:** Ex-paladins who become Corrupt Avengers retain modified paladin abilities.
- **Notes:** Single-minded vengeance archetype. Works well as a morally complex NPC or player character embracing taint purposefully. Good gateway to exploring corruption arc in Ptolus — e.g., a Knight of the Pale fallen to obsession.

### Death Delver
- **Source:** HoH p. 93
- **Requirements:** Base Will save +2; Concentration 8 ranks; Heal 2 ranks; Knowledge (religion) 4 ranks; must have been dropped below 0 hp and survived
- **HD:** d8 | **BAB:** Medium | **Saves:** Fort good, Ref poor, Will good
- **Length:** 10 levels
- **Spellcasting:** Divine, Wisdom-based, prepared (like cleric). Slower progression — 1st level spells start at level 1, 4th level spells at level 10. Regains spells via 1-hour nightly meditation (not sleep). Spontaneous cure or inflict (choose at 2nd level).
- **Key Features:**
  - **Deathsense** (Ex, 1st): Deathwatch at will as a swift action.
  - **Rebuke Undead** (1st): As evil cleric, using Death Delver level.
  - **Deadened Soul** (Ex, 2nd): Immune to all fear effects.
  - **Death Ward** (Su, 3rd, 1/day → 3/day): As the spell but 1 hour/class level.
  - **Diehard** (4th): Gains feat even without prerequisites.
  - **Fear Aura** (Su, 5th, 1/day): 30-ft. radius; Will save (DC 10 + level + Cha) or panicked (as fear spell) for class level rounds.
  - **Cheat Death** (Ex, 7th): Automatically succeeds on saves from coup de grace and massive damage Fortitude saves (can still die from damage itself).
  - **Mantle of Life** (Su, 8th): Full-round action; all allies within 60 ft. gain +4 Will saves for 1 min/level.
  - **Nine Lives** (Ex, 10th): Nine times per career, instead of dying (reduced below –10 hp or otherwise killed by a single effect), stabilize at –9 hp and lose one "life."
- **Notes:** Does not require taint mechanics at all — can be used in any campaign. Excellent for a character who survives something terrible in Ptolus (a near-death in the Deep Dungeon, an encounter with a Galchutt manifestation). Society of the Sacred Shroud is a minor order without ties to any specific deity — fits a non-denominational Ptolus approach.

### Dread Witch
- **Source:** HoH p. 98
- **Requirements:** Base Will save +4; Knowledge (arcana) 3 ranks; can cast cause fear and scare; must have failed at least one fear save
- **HD:** d4 | **BAB:** Poor | **Saves:** Fort poor, Ref poor, Will good
- **Length:** 5 levels
- **Spellcasting:** +1 level to existing spellcasting class at levels 2–5 (loses one level of spellcasting progression).
- **Key Features:**
  - **Master of Terror** (1st, Ex): +1 to save DCs of all fear spells (stacks with Spell Focus). Add bane and doom to arcane spell list as 2nd-level spells. +2 per class level to Intimidate.
  - **Unnatural Will** (1st): Bonus feat (even without prerequisites).
  - **Absorb Fear** (2nd, Su): When subject to a fear effect, gains +1/+2/+3 CL (shaken/frightened/panicked) or can use as free extra spell slot (cantrip/1st/2nd). Gains the bonus even on a successful save.
  - **Fearful Empowerment** (3rd, Su, 1/day → 2/day at 5th): Add [Fear] descriptor to any spell with a visual manifestation. All creatures in area make Will save (DC 10 + class level + Cha) or shaken 1d4 rounds.
  - **Delay Fear** (4th, Su): Delay onset of fear effects imposed on others (up to Cha mod minutes). Also delay feared status affecting self (Cha mod rounds, twice/day), while still gaining Absorb Fear benefits.
  - **Greater Master of Terror** (4th, Ex): Fear spell DCs increase from +1 to +2; fear spells can affect normally fear-immune creatures (e.g., paladins), unless target's HD exceeds caster level by 4+.
  - **Horrific Aura** (5th, Su): Passively shakes creatures of 6 HD or less within 10 ft. (Will save). Can channel into touch attack (Will save or panicked 1d4+1 rounds) Cha modifier times/day; touch works on any HD, including fear-immune.
  - **Reflective Fear** (5th, Su): On a successful save vs. a fear effect, the fear is reflected back at the source (source makes same save or becomes subject).
- **Notes:** Bard or sorcerer multiclass is most efficient. No taint dependency; pure fear manipulation. In Ptolus, could represent a character who studied with the Malkuth (mind-controlling cult) or simply a survivor who learned to weaponize dread.

### Fiend-Blooded
- **Source:** HoH p. 102
- **Requirements:** Any humanoid race (not already a half-fiend); nongood alignment; Concentration 8 ranks; Knowledge (arcana) 8 ranks; Knowledge (the planes) 8 ranks; Blood Calls to Blood feat; Eschew Materials feat; able to cast 2nd-level arcane spells without preparation
- **HD:** d4 | **BAB:** Medium | **Saves:** Fort poor, Ref poor, Will good
- **Length:** 10 levels (loses spellcasting at level 10 for Fiendish Exaltation)
- **Spellcasting:** +1 level to existing arcane spellcasting class at levels 1–9.
- **Key Features:**
  - **Fiendish Heritage** (passive, scaling): +1 natural armor (1st); +1 Cha (3rd); +1 Int (5th); +1 Dex (7th); +1 Con (9th).
  - **Fiendish Companion** (1st): May apply fiendish template to familiar.
  - **Fiendish Sorcery** (2nd, 4th, 6th, 8th, 10th): At each of these levels, add one spell known from: spells with the [Fire] descriptor, or spells from enchantment, illusion, or necromancy schools (including normally divine spells).
  - **Blood of Fiends** (3rd, Ex): +1 on saves vs. poison, –1 to poison damage. At 6th: resist cold/fire 5. At 9th: resist acid/electricity 5.
  - **Smiting Spell** (4th, Su, 1/day → 2/day at 8th): Free action; chosen damaging spell deals +class level + Cha modifier extra damage to all in area.
  - **Fiendish Exaltation** (10th, Ex): Additional +1 Dex/Con/Int, +2 Str (stacking with prior Fiendish Heritage boosts); immunity to poison; DR 10/magic. No spellcasting advancement at this level.
- **Notes:** Strong sorcerer/bard multiclass. In Ptolus, Fiend-Blooded characters could tie to the Chaosborn (tieflings), the House of Ptolus's various demonic incursion histories, or a character whose ancestor made a deal during the Dark Chaos. The "house" organization mechanic is optional and fits noble families of the city.

### Purifier of the Hallowed Doctrine
- **Source:** HoH p. 108
- **Requirements:** Any good or true neutral; Knowledge (arcana) 4 ranks; Knowledge (religion) 8 ranks; Pure Soul feat; ability to turn undead
- **HD:** d8 | **BAB:** Medium | **Saves:** Fort good, Ref poor, Will good
- **Length:** 10 levels
- **Spellcasting:** +1 level to existing divine spellcasting class at levels 1, 3, 5, 7, 9 (odd levels only; 5 total over 10 levels).
- **Key Features:**
  - **Aura of Purity** (Ex, 1st): Tainted creatures can detect you with a Spot check (DC 20 – class level).
  - **Detect Taint** (Sp, 1st): At will (as the spell, HoH p. 128).
  - **Turn Undead** (1st): Purifier levels stack with cleric/paladin levels for turning.
  - **Lay on Hands** (Su, 2nd): As paladin of class level (stacks with paladin levels).
  - **Smite Taint** (Su, 3rd, 1/day → 3/day at 9th): As paladin smite evil but against tainted creatures (can redirect other smite abilities).
  - **Aura of Sanctity** (Su, 4th): All allies within 10 ft. gain +2 sacred bonus on saves to resist acquiring taint (becomes +4 at 10th).
  - **Turn Tainted** (Su, 5th): Can turn any tainted creature as if it were an undead of HD +4.
  - **Purify Soul** (Su, 6th): Spend a turn undead use to touch a living being and cure 1 point of Corruption or Depravity (unwilling: Fort for Corruption, Will for Depravity, DC 10 + level + Cha). Cannot drop below a threshold this way. Innately tainted creatures are unaffected. Improves to 2 pts/touch (8th) and 3 pts/touch (10th).
  - **Cleansing Flame** (Su, 7th, 3/day): Any offensive spell cast against a tainted creature is automatically Maximized (no higher slot needed; functions normally vs. non-tainted creatures in area).
  - **Shield of Purity** (Su, 8th): Spend a turn undead use; tainted creatures cannot enter a 10-ft. radius around you; all within gain +6 sacred bonus on saves vs. tainted creatures' spells/magical attacks. Lasts Cha modifier rounds (min 1).
- **Covenant of the Hallowed Doctrine:** Multi-faith organization (clerics, paladins, others). Maintains monasteries. Provides training, information, backup, and certain magic items (healing/restoration gear, rod of sanctity). Ranks: Acolyte, Disciple, Archimandrite.
- **Notes:** Ideal for Ptolus. The Banewarrens, Deep Dungeon, and Galchutt cult threat create exactly the kind of taint-heavy environment this class is designed for. Could represent a splinter arm of the Knights of the Pale, the Church of Asche, or an independent multi-faith order.

### Tainted Scholar
- **Source:** HoH p. 113
- **Requirements:** Concentration 8 ranks; Knowledge (arcana) 4 ranks; ability to cast 1st-level arcane spells (or warlock least invocations); Depravity score moderate or greater
- **HD:** d6 | **BAB:** Poor | **Saves:** Fort poor, Ref poor, Will good
- **Length:** 10 levels (may be trapped in this class — see Level Advancement)
- **Spellcasting:** +1 level to existing arcane spellcasting class every level (full arcane progression).
- **Key Features:**
  - **Tainted Spellcasting** (1st, Su): Replaces normal spellcasting ability. Use Depravity score in place of key ability (bonus spells based on 10 + Depravity; DC 10 + spell level + ½ Corruption). Must have Depravity ≥ spell level to cast. Each arcane spell cast: Will save DC 10 + spell level or +1 Depravity. All arcane spells are evil spells.
  - **Blood Component** (1st, Su): Substitute blood (1 hp damage, free action) for a spell's material component, increasing spell's CL by 1.
  - **Level Advancement** (1st, ongoing): To take a level in another class, must succeed on Will save DC 10 + Depravity score. Failure forces another Tainted Scholar level.
  - **Taint Suppression** (1st): Same as Corrupt Avenger — replaces physical symptoms with Internal Corruption.
  - **Secrets** (2nd, 4th, 6th, 8th, 10th): Choose one per milestone from a list (minimum level + Depravity determines eligibility):
    - *Blooded Metamagic:* Apply a metamagic feat by paying Con damage equal to slot increase.
    - *Stanch:* Auto-stabilize below 0 hp.
    - *Death Knell:* 1/day spell-like ability.
    - *Clarity of True Madness:* 1/day add Depravity as bonus to one Wis-based check or Will save.
    - *Wholesome Corruption:* 1/day add Corruption as bonus to one Concentration check or Fort save.
    - *Bloodseeking Spell:* Pay 3 hp; chosen damaging spell deals +1d6 damage to all targets (not undead/constructs/bloodless).
    - *Thicker Than Water:* DR 1/piercing and slashing.
    - *Tainted Knowledge:* Gain a bonus tainted feat.
    - *Forbidden Arcana:* Extra 1st-level spell slot/day.
    - *Greater Forbidden Arcana:* Extra 2nd-level spell slot/day.
  - **Lore** (3rd): Bardic knowledge using class level + Int (or Depravity for forbidden/tainted topics).
  - **Outré Lore** (5th, Sp, 1/day): Contact other plane as spell-like ability; failure to make Int check also requires Will save or +1 Depravity.
  - **Aberrant Lore** (7th): Full-round action to sense whether a creature/object/location within 60 ft. carries taint and its severity; also can identify magic items (as identify, 1 hour) and receive visions of past events at tainted locations (1 hour).
  - **Heretical Lore** (9th, 1/day): Grants limited wish from planar contacts (no XP cost), but +1d3 to both Corruption and Depravity, plus a geas/quest to perform an evil act.
- **Notes:** Mechanically entangled with taint — progressively harder to abandon. Designed for dread necromancers and specialist wizards. In Ptolus, fits the Dreaming Apothecary, rogue mages studying Galchutt artifacts, or chaositech researchers who crossed a line.

## New Spells (Summary — HoH Chapter 5)

**Detect Taint** (Cleric 1, Druid 1, Purifier): Cone 60 ft.; reveals taint strength and location over 3 rounds.
**Resist Taint** (Cleric 3, Druid 3, Paladin 3, Ranger 3): +4 sacred bonus on saves to resist acquiring taint.
**Summon Undead I–V** (arcane and divine lists): Summons undead creatures instead of fiends/celestials.
**Rigor Mortis** (Cleric 3, Death Delver 2): Subject appears dead; all vital functions suspended.

**Evil Spells and Taint variant:** Casting [Evil] descriptor spells → Will DC 10 + spell level or +1 Depravity.

**Corrupt Spells (selective list):**
- *Call Forth the Beast* (Corrupt 5): Target awakens chaotic evil on next sleep; doesn't remember anything after. Corruption cost: 1d4 Con damage.
- *Chain of Sorrow* (Corrupt 7): 2d10 Cha drain that passes like a chain through allies of target. Corruption cost: 2d4 Cha damage.

## New Feats (Notable — HoH Chapter 5)

**Tainted Feats** (require taint scores):
- **Corrupt Arcana** (mild Depravity): Spontaneous casters can prepare and cast corrupt spells.
- **Tainted Fury** (moderate Corruption): Once/day, spend a use of turn undead to gain temporary HP and a smite-like melee bonus for 30 rounds.
- **Touch of Taint** (mild Corruption): Melee touch attack bestows 1 point of Corruption or Depravity on target (Fort/Will DC 10 + Corruption/Depravity score).
- **Surge of Malevolence** (moderate Corruption or Depravity): Once/day, add higher taint score as bonus on one attack roll, save, or check.

**Vile Feats** (evil alignment):
- **Willing Deformity** (evil alignment): +3 Intimidate; can take Deformity sub-feats.
- **Deformity (Skin/Tall/Teeth/Tongue):** Various bonuses in exchange for disturbing mutations.

**General:**
- **Pure Soul** (Purifier prerequisite): +4 bonus on saves vs. taint; immune to fear effects with [Fear] descriptor (not general fear-based effects).
- **Eldritch Corruption**: Your arcane spells carry a chance of bestowing taint on the target.
- **Bane Magic**: Spells deal +1 damage/die vs. your designated foe type.
- **Spirit Sense**: Detect incorporeal creatures within 30 ft. (like blindsense, incorporeal only).
- **Forbidden Lore**: +4 to Knowledge (arcana) and Knowledge (religion); can identify tainted items/creatures as a full-round action.
- **Unnatural Will**: +4 on saves vs. fear effects; immune to shaken condition.

## Magic Items (HoH)

### Rod of Sanctity
- **Source:** HoH p. 133
- **Description:** Holy weapon (functions as +2 holy mace). When striking tainted creatures, deals +2d6 extra damage. Wielder is immune to acquiring taint (Corruption or Depravity) from any source while holding the rod. Unique to the Covenant of the Hallowed Doctrine; treated as organizational property.
- **Notes:** Extremely valuable in a Ptolus campaign. The Covenant may lend one to a party about to enter the Banewarrens.

### Artifacts (Malign)
- **Acererak's Robe:** Wearer can convert living creatures to undead with a touch (full description HoH p. 133–134).
- Two additional malign artifacts described in book (DM reference only).
