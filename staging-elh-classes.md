# Epic Class Progressions
**Source:** Epic Level Handbook (ELH) Chapter 1, pp. 8–23

All classes: base attack and base save bonuses stop at 20th level; use Table 1-1 epic bonuses instead. Spells per day do not increase after 20th level. All scaling class features (sneak attack, rage uses, etc.) continue at the same rate.

---

## Barbarian
- **Epic bonus feat:** Every 4 levels after 20 (24, 28, 32...)
- **Additional rage:** +1 use/day every 4 levels after 20 (24, 28, 32...)
- **Damage reduction:** +1 every 3 levels after 20 (23=DR 5/–, 26=DR 6/–, 29=DR 7/–...)
- **Uncanny dodge trap bonus:** +1 every 3 levels after 19 (22, 25, 28...)
- **Bonus feat list:** Armor Skin, Chaotic Rage, Damage Reduction, Devastating Critical, Dire Charge, Epic Endurance, Epic Prowess, Epic Speed, Epic Toughness, Epic Weapon Focus, Fast Healing, Incite Rage, Legendary Climber/Leaper/Rider/Tracker/Wrestler, Mighty Rage, Overwhelming Critical, Ruinous Rage, Terrifying Rage, Thundering Rage

## Monk
- **Epic bonus feat:** Every 5 levels after 20 (25, 30, 35...)
- **AC bonus:** +1 every 5 levels after 20 (25=+5, 30=+6...)
- **Unarmored speed:** +10 ft. at 21st level and every 3 levels after (24, 27, 30...); Small/Dwarf monks gain +5 ft. instead
- **Unarmed damage:** Does NOT increase after 16th level
- **Ki strike:** Does not auto-improve; requires Improved Ki Strike epic feat
- **Diamond Soul:** SR = class level + 10 (continues scaling)
- **Bonus feat list:** Armor Skin, Blinding Speed, Damage Reduction, Energy Resistance, Epic Prowess/Speed/Toughness, Exceptional Deflection, Fast Healing, Improved Combat Reflexes, Improved Ki Strike, Improved Spell Resistance, Improved Stunning Fist, Infinite Deflection, Keen Strike, Legendary Climber/Wrestler, Reflect Arrows, Righteous Strike, Self-Concealment, Shattering Strike, Vorpal Strike

## Paladin
- **Epic bonus feat:** Every 3 levels after 20 (23, 26, 29...)
- **Remove disease:** +1 use/week every 3 levels after 18 (21=7/week, 24=8/week...)
- **Smite evil:** Add class level to damage (continues scaling)
- **Lay on hands:** Charisma bonus × class level (continues scaling)
- **Special mount:** +2 HD, +2 natural armor, +1 Str, +1 Int every 5 levels after 20 (25, 30...); SR = class level + 5
- **Spells:** Caster level = 1/2 class level; no new spells/day
- **Bonus feat list:** Armor Skin, Devastating Critical, Epic Leadership, Epic Prowess, Epic Reputation, Epic Toughness, Epic Weapon Focus, Great Smiting, Holy Strike, Improved Aura of Courage, Improved Combat Casting, Improved Spell Capacity, Legendary Commander/Rider, Overwhelming Critical, Perfect Health, Permanent Emanation, Planar Turning, Positive Energy Aura, Spectral Strike, Spontaneous Spell, Widen Aura of Courage

## Wizard
- **Epic bonus feat:** Every 3 levels after 20 (23, 26, 29...)
- **Familiar:** +1 natural armor and +1 Int every 2 levels after 20; SR = master level + 5
- **Spells:** Learns 2 new spells of any castable level each new level gained; no new spell slots
