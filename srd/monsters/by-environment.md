## Ptolus Dungeon (everything goes)

Mix of humanoids, undead, aberrations, oozes, demons. Most monsters in the database tag this environment. Common picks by CR band:

- **CR 1/4–1/2**: Kobold, Goblin, Hobgoblin, Orc, Skeleton, Zombie, Stirge
- **CR 1**: Drow Warrior, Giant Spider, Ghoul, Darkmantle, Grimlock, Troglodyte, Duergar, Shrieker, Lemure
- **CR 2**: Bugbear, Choker, Dretch, Doppelganger, Rat Swarm, Bat Swarm
- **CR 3**: Shadow, Wight, Allip, Ghast, Rust Monster, Gelatinous Cube, Violet Fungus, Grick, Hell Hound
- **CR 4**: Gray Ooze, Carrion Crawler, Mimic, Otyugh, Vampire Spawn
- **CR 5**: Mummy, Wraith, Ochre Jelly, Gibbering Mouther
- **CR 6**: Babau, Chain Devil
- **CR 7**: Spectre, Black Pudding, Aboleth, Succubus, Drider
- **CR 8**: Mind Flayer, Bodak, Mohrg, Vampire, Stone Giant, Bone Devil, Erinyes
- **CR 9+**: Vrock, Bone Devil, Hezrou, Beholder
