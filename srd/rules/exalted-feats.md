# Book of Exalted Deeds — Exalted Feats

Source: BoED Chapter 4 (pp. 39–48)

All feats below require good alignment (and DM permission) unless noted as [GENERAL] or [METAMAGIC]. [EXALTED] feats are supernatural gifts from celestial powers; losing good alignment or willfully committing an evil act causes immediate loss of all exalted feat benefits until atonement.

---

## General Feats (non-exalted, but thematically related)

**Ancestral Relic** [GENERAL] — Invest an inherited masterwork item with increasing magic power by sacrificing gold at a consecrated site. | Prereq: Any good alignment, character level 3rd

**Consecrate Spell-Like Ability** [GENERAL] — Adds the good descriptor to one spell-like ability; half damage bypasses energy immunity. | Prereq: Any good alignment

**Purify Spell-Like Ability** [GENERAL] — Adds the good descriptor to one spell-like ability; neutral creatures take half damage, good creatures take none, evil outsiders take extra (damage die steps up). | Prereq: Any good alignment

**Resounding Blow** [GENERAL] — On a critical hit with a melee weapon, target must save (Will DC 10 + ½ level + Cha) or cower for 1 round. | Prereq: Str 13, Power Attack, Intimidate 7 ranks *(Fighter bonus feat)*

**Spell Focus (Good)** [GENERAL] — +2 to save DCs for all spells with the good descriptor. | Prereq: Any good alignment

**Subduing Strike** [GENERAL] — No penalty on attack rolls when dealing nonlethal damage; rogues may sneak attack for nonlethal with normal melee weapons. | Prereq: — *(Fighter bonus feat)*

## Metamagic Feats

**Consecrate Spell** [METAMAGIC] — Spell gains the good descriptor; half the damage bypasses energy immunity. +1 spell level. | Prereq: Any good alignment

**Nonlethal Substitution** [METAMAGIC] — Convert a spell's energy damage (acid, cold, electricity, fire, or sonic) to nonlethal damage. +0 spell levels. | Prereq: Any other metamagic feat, Knowledge (arcana) 5 ranks

**Purify Spell** [METAMAGIC] — Spell gains the good descriptor; neutral creatures take half damage, good creatures take none, evil outsiders take extra damage (die steps up). +1 spell level. | Prereq: Any good alignment

## Exalted Feats

**Animal Friend** [EXALTED] — +4 exalted bonus on wild empathy checks to influence animals and good-aligned magical beasts. | Prereq: Cha 15, wild empathy class feature

**Celestial Familiar** [EXALTED] — Gain access to celestial creatures (celestial animals, coure eladrin, lantern archon, musteval guardinal) as familiars. | Prereq: Able to acquire a new familiar, minimum level by familiar type (3rd–7th)

**Celestial Mount** [EXALTED] — Paladin's special mount gains the celestial creature template (smite evil 1/day, darkvision, energy resistance, DR, SR scaling with HD). | Prereq: Paladin level 4th

**Consecrate Spell Trigger** [EXALTED] — Expend a turning attempt when activating a wand or staff to apply the Consecrate Spell effect to the triggered spell. | Prereq: Craft Wand or Craft Staff, ability to turn undead

**Exalted Companion** [EXALTED] — Gain access to magical beasts of good alignment (celestial animals, blink dog, asperi, giant eagle, giant owl, pegasus, unicorn) as animal companions. | Prereq: Able to acquire a new animal companion, minimum druid level by companion type

**Exalted Smite** [EXALTED] — When using smite evil, the weapon is treated as good-aligned for bypassing damage reduction. | Prereq: Smite evil class ability

**Exalted Spell Resistance** [EXALTED] — Spell resistance increases by +4 against evil spells and spell-like abilities of evil outsiders. | Prereq: Cha 15, spell resistance

**Exalted Turning** [EXALTED] — Any undead you turn also takes 3d6 points of damage in addition to normal turning effect. | Prereq: Ability to turn undead

**Exalted Wild Shape** [EXALTED] — Wild shape can now produce the form of a blink dog, giant eagle, giant owl, pegasus, unicorn, or any celestial animal you could normally assume. | Prereq: Wild shape class ability, wild shaping class level 8th

**Favored of the Companions** [EXALTED] — Once per day while performing an act of good, gain a +1 luck bonus on any one roll or check (allegiance to Talisid/Five Companions). | Prereq: — *(Mutually exclusive with Servant of the Heavens and Knight of Stars)*

**Fist of the Heavens** [EXALTED] — Stunning Fist DC +2 against evil creatures; on success the creature is also staggered for 1 round following the round it is stunned. | Prereq: Wis 15, Sanctify Ki Strike, Stunning Fist *(Fighter bonus feat)*

**Gift of Faith** [EXALTED] — +2 bonus on saves against fear effects, despair effects, and similar mind-affecting conditions (not charms or compulsions). | Prereq: Wis 13

**Gift of Grace** [EXALTED] — Sacrifice any portion of your divine grace saving throw bonus and distribute it among touched allies (max one ally per character level); lasts 1 day or until revoked. | Prereq: Divine grace class ability

**Hands of a Healer** [EXALTED] — When calculating lay on hands healing, treat Charisma as 2 points higher. | Prereq: Cha 13, lay on hands class ability

**Holy Ki Strike** [EXALTED] — Unarmed strikes deal +2d6 holy damage to evil creatures and are treated as holy for DR purposes. | Prereq: Cha 15, Improved Unarmed Strike, Ki strike (holy), Sanctify Ki Strike

**Holy Radiance** [EXALTED] — As a free action, blaze with bright light (10-ft radius); undead within 10 feet take 1d4 damage per round. | Prereq: Cha 15, Nimbus of Light

**Holy Subdual** [EXALTED] — When dealing nonlethal damage, convert bonus damage from a holy weapon, smite evil, or smite granted power to nonlethal damage as well. | Prereq: Subduing Strike *(Fighter bonus feat)*

**Intuitive Attack** [EXALTED] — Use Wisdom modifier instead of Strength on attack rolls with simple weapons and natural weapons. | Prereq: Base attack bonus +1 *(Fighter bonus feat)*

**Knight of Stars** [EXALTED] — Once per day while performing an act of good, gain a +1 luck bonus on any one roll or check (allegiance to the Court of Stars/eladrin). | Prereq: — *(Mutually exclusive with Servant of the Heavens and Favored of the Companions)*

**Nemesis** [EXALTED] — Detect a chosen favored enemy type by supernatural sense within 60 feet (distance + direction, through walls); also deal +1d6 damage against evil members of that type. | Prereq: Favored enemy class ability *(Can be taken multiple times for different favored enemies)*

**Nimbus of Light** [EXALTED] — Glow with soft light; gain +2 bonus on Diplomacy and Sense Motive checks with good-aligned creatures. | Prereq: —

**Nymph's Kiss** [EXALTED] — Good-aligned fey regard you as fey; +2 circumstance bonus on Charisma-related checks, +1 bonus on saves against spells and spell-like abilities, +1 skill point per level from the level you take this feat. | Prereq: — *(Must maintain intimate relationship with a good-aligned fey)*

**Purify Spell Trigger** [EXALTED] — Expend a turning attempt when activating a wand or staff to apply the Purify Spell effect (neutral half, good none, evil outsiders extra). | Prereq: Craft Wand or Craft Staff, ability to turn undead

**Quell the Profane** [EXALTED] — On a critical hit against an evil creature, target must save (Fort DC 10 + ½ level + Cha) or take 1d4+1 temporary Strength damage. | Prereq: Str 13, Power Attack, Resounding Blow, BAB +8

**Ranged Smite Evil** [EXALTED] — Channel smite evil through a ranged weapon attack; counts as one daily use of smite evil. | Prereq: Smite evil class ability

**Righteous Wrath** [EXALTED] — First melee hit on an evil foe during rage forces a Will save (DC 10 + ½ raging class level + Cha) or the target is shaken; while raging you retain full clarity to deal nonlethal damage and show mercy. | Prereq: Rage class ability

**Sacred Strike** [EXALTED] — Sneak attacks against evil creatures roll d8s instead of d6s and the damage counts as good-aligned for bypassing DR. | Prereq: Sneak attack class ability

**Sacred Vow** [EXALTED] — +2 perfection bonus on Diplomacy checks; prerequisite for all Vow feats. | Prereq: — *(No alignment requirement beyond what exalted feats imply)*

**Sanctify Ki Strike** [EXALTED] — Unarmed strikes deal +1 damage to evil creatures (+1d4 to evil outsiders and evil undead) and are treated as good-aligned for DR. | Prereq: Improved Unarmed Strike, Ki strike (lawful), Cha 15

**Sanctify Martial Strike** [EXALTED] — Attacks with one chosen weapon deal +1 damage to evil creatures (+1d4 vs. evil outsiders/undead) and are treated as good-aligned for DR. *(Can be taken multiple times for different weapons.)* | Prereq: Cha 15, Weapon Focus with specified weapon

**Sanctify Natural Attack** [EXALTED] — Natural attacks deal +1 damage to evil creatures (+1d4 vs. evil outsiders/undead) and are treated as good-aligned for DR. | Prereq: One or more natural weapon attacks, BAB +5

**Sanctify Weapon** [EXALTED] — When you cast align weapon, the target weapon is also sanctified: +1 damage to evil creatures (+1d4 vs. evil outsiders/undead); corrupted creatures cannot heal damage dealt by it normally. | Prereq: Able to cast align weapon

**Servant of the Heavens** [EXALTED] — Once per day while performing an act of good, gain a +1 luck bonus on any one roll or check (allegiance to the Celestial Hebdomad/tome archons). | Prereq: — *(Mutually exclusive with Knight of Stars and Favored of the Companions)*

**Stigmata** [EXALTED] — As a free action, take at least 2 temporary Constitution damage to heal allies: 1 hp per level per ally per 2 Con damage taken; also grants a sacred bonus on disease saves equal to Con damage taken. Bleeding lasts 1 hour; Con damage cannot be healed until bleeding stops. | Prereq: Nimbus of Light

**Touch of Golden Ice** [EXALTED] — Any evil creature touched with your bare hand, fist, or natural weapon is exposed to the ravage golden ice (initial 1 Con, secondary 1d6 Con, Fort DC 13). | Prereq: Con 13

**Vow of Abstinence** [EXALTED] — +4 perfection bonus on Fort saves against poisons and drugs (if subjected unwillingly). | Prereq: Sacred Vow *(Breaking intentionally = permanent loss; breaking via compulsion requires atonement)*

**Vow of Chastity** [EXALTED] — +4 perfection bonus on Will saves against charm and phantasm spells and effects. | Prereq: Sacred Vow *(Must abstain from all sexual contact)*

**Vow of Nonviolence** [EXALTED] — +4 bonus on save DCs of nondamaging spells and special abilities against humanoid and monstrous humanoid targets; allies who kill helpless foes within 120 ft. suffer a morale penalty. | Prereq: Sacred Vow *(Must not deal damage to humanoids/monstrous humanoids)*

**Vow of Obedience** [EXALTED] — +4 perfection bonus on Will saves against compulsion spells and effects. | Prereq: Sacred Vow *(Must obey superior in religious order unquestioningly)*

**Vow of Peace** [EXALTED] — Constant calm emotions aura (20-ft radius, Will save DC 10 + ½ level + Cha); +2 natural armor, +2 deflection, +2 exalted AC; weapons striking you may shatter (Fort save); +4 exalted bonus on Diplomacy. | Prereq: Sacred Vow, Vow of Nonviolence *(Must not harm any living creature)*

**Vow of Poverty** [EXALTED] — Forswear all material possessions (see full table below) in exchange for escalating supernatural bonuses to AC, ability scores, saves, and DR; also grants bonus exalted feats. | Prereq: Sacred Vow *(Breaking = permanent loss, no replacement feat)*

**Vow of Purity** [EXALTED] — +4 perfection bonus on Fort saves against disease and death effects. | Prereq: Sacred Vow *(Must avoid all contact with dead flesh)*

**Words of Creation** [EXALTED] — Ability to use the Words of Creation to enhance bardic music, good spellcasting, magic item creation, and acts of creation (see BoED Chapter 2 for full rules). | Prereq: Int 15, Cha 15, base Will save bonus +5

## Vow of Poverty

### What it requires

You must own nothing beyond: ordinary (non-magic, non-masterwork) simple weapons (typically a quarterstaff); simple clothing (homespun robe, hat, sandals) with no magical properties; enough food for one day in a simple non-magic sack; a spell component pouch. You may not use any magic item yourself, though you may benefit from magic items used on your behalf (potions given to you, spells cast from a wand by an ally, etc.). You may not "borrow" a magic item even for one round.

**Breaking the vow:** Immediately and irrevocably lose all benefits. You may not take another feat to replace Vow of Poverty.

### Benefit progression by character level

| Character Level | Benefit |
|----------------|---------|
| 1st | AC bonus +4 (exalted, no touch/incorporeal), bonus exalted feat |
| 2nd | Bonus exalted feat |
| 3rd | AC bonus +5, endure elements (immune to environmental heat/cold, –50°F to 140°F) |
| 4th | Exalted strike +1 (weapon treated as +1 magic), bonus exalted feat |
| 5th | Sustenance (no need to eat or drink) |
| 6th | AC bonus +6, deflection +1, bonus exalted feat |
| 7th | Resistance +1 (all saves), ability score enhancement +2 (one score) |
| 8th | Natural armor +1, mind shielding (immune to detect thoughts, discern lies, alignment detection), bonus exalted feat |
| 9th | AC bonus +7 |
| 10th | Exalted strike +2 (weapon also good-aligned), damage reduction 5/magic, bonus exalted feat |
| 11th | Ability score enhancement +4/+2 (first score +4, pick second at +2) |
| 12th | AC bonus +8, deflection +2, greater sustenance (no need to breathe), bonus exalted feat |
| 13th | Resistance +2, energy resistance 5 (acid/cold/electricity/fire/sonic) |
| 14th | Exalted strike +3, freedom of movement (continuous), bonus exalted feat |
| 15th | AC bonus +9, ability score enhancement +6/+4/+2 (pick third score at +2), damage reduction 5/evil |
| 16th | Natural armor +2, bonus exalted feat |
| 17th | Exalted strike +4, resistance +3, regeneration (1 hp per level per hour; nonlethal heals 1/level per 5 min) |
| 18th | AC bonus +10, deflection +3, true seeing (continuous), bonus exalted feat |
| 19th | Ability score enhancement +8/+6/+4/+2 (pick fourth score at +2), damage reduction 10/evil |
| 20th | Exalted strike +5, energy resistance 15, bonus exalted feat |

**Notes on Vow of Peace interaction:** If you also have Vow of Peace, the natural armor, deflection, and exalted AC bonuses from Vow of Peace each increase by +2.

**Bonus exalted feats:** Unlike other benefits, bonus feats are NOT gained retroactively when you take the feat at a higher level — only feats tied to levels you gain *after* swearing the vow are available. The 1st-level feat is only available to a human who takes both Sacred Vow and Vow of Poverty at character level 1.
