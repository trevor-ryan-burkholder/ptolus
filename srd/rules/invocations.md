# Warlock Invocations (Complete Arcane)

Source: *Complete Arcane* (CA), Chapter 1 (class description pp. 5–18) and invocation descriptions pp. 131–145.

Warlocks know a set number of invocations (see class table) and may use each **at will** with no spell slots. Invocations are **spell-like abilities** (not spells), subject to SR and dispel magic, but cannot be counterspelled. Standard action to use unless stated. Save DCs = 10 + equivalent spell level + Cha modifier.

Invocations are divided into four grades by the minimum warlock level required to select them.

---

## Eldritch Blast (Core Ability)

Not an invocation — this is the warlock's signature ability, scaling automatically.

| Warlock Level | Blast Damage | Range |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | 1d6 | 60 ft. |
| 3rd | 2d6 | 60 ft. |
| 5th | 3d6 | 60 ft. |
| 7th | 4d6 | 60 ft. |
| 9th | 5d6 | 60 ft. |
| 11th | 6d6 | 60 ft. |
| 13th | 7d6 | 60 ft. |
| 15th | 8d6 | 60 ft. |
| 17th | 9d6 | 60 ft. |
| 19th | 10d6 | 60 ft. |

Eldritch Essence invocations modify the blast's damage type or add effects. Blast Shape invocations change the delivery (line, cone, etc.).

## Least Invocations (Warlock 1+)

These can be selected starting at 1st level.

### Eldritch Spear *(Blast Shape)*
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 2nd  
Eldritch blast range extends to **250 ft.** No other changes.

### Hideous Blow *(Blast Shape)*
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 2nd  
Deliver eldritch blast as part of a melee weapon attack. The blast is delivered on a hit; the weapon also deals its normal damage.

### Sickening Blast *(Eldritch Essence)*
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 2nd  
Target hit by eldritch blast must make a **Fort save** or become **sickened** for 1 minute.

### Beguiling Influence
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 2nd | **Duration:** 24 hours  
Gain a **+6 bonus on Bluff, Diplomacy, and Intimidate** checks.

### Dark One's Own Luck
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 2nd | **Duration:** 24 hours  
Add your **Cha modifier** (minimum +0) as a **luck bonus** on one type of saving throw (chosen when invocation is activated: Fort, Ref, or Will).

### Detect Magic
**Equiv. Spell Level:** —  
As the spell *detect magic*, at will, with no duration limit. A warlock may use this continuously as a free action.

### Leaps and Bounds
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 2nd | **Duration:** 24 hours  
Gain a **+4 bonus on Balance, Jump, and Tumble** checks.

### Miasmic Cloud
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 2nd | **Duration:** Concentration + 1 round  
Create a **15-ft.-radius cloud** of shadowy vapor (as *obscuring mist*). Creatures in the cloud are **nauseated** on a failed Fort save (DC 10 + Cha).

### See the Unseen
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 2nd | **Duration:** 24 hours  
Gain **darkvision 60 ft.** and **see invisibility** (as the spell) continuously.

### Spiderwalk
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 2nd | **Duration:** 24 hours  
Gain **spider climb** (as the spell); move on walls and ceilings at half speed.

### Summon Swarm
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 2nd | **Duration:** Concentration  
As *summon swarm*. Summon a swarm of bats, rats, or spiders that occupies the target area.

### Frightful Blast *(Eldritch Essence)*
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 3rd  
Target hit by eldritch blast must make a **Will save** or become **shaken** for 1 minute. (Shaken, not frightened — cannot stack to fear.)

## Lesser Invocations (Warlock 6+)

These can be selected starting at 6th level. A warlock must already have a Least invocation to take a Lesser one.

### Eldritch Chain *(Blast Shape)*
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 4th  
Eldritch blast arcs to one secondary target within 30 ft. of the first for half damage. At 11th level, it can chain to a second secondary target for quarter damage.

### Eldritch Cone *(Blast Shape)*
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 4th  
Eldritch blast becomes a **30-ft. cone** (Reflex half). All creatures in area take damage.

### Beshadowed Blast *(Eldritch Essence)*
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 4th  
Target hit by eldritch blast must make a **Fort save** or become **blinded** for 1 minute.

### Brimstone Blast *(Eldritch Essence)*
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 4th  
Eldritch blast deals **fire damage** (instead of untyped). Targets that fail a **Ref save** also catch fire, taking 2d6 fire damage per round until extinguished.

### Hellrime Blast *(Eldritch Essence)*
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 4th  
Eldritch blast deals **cold damage** (instead of untyped). Targets that fail a **Fort save** take a **−4 penalty to Dex** for 10 minutes (does not stack).

### Vitriolic Blast *(Eldritch Essence)*
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 4th  
Eldritch blast deals **acid damage** that bypasses **spell resistance**. In addition, targets take 2d6 acid damage per round for 2 rounds (no save to negate the ongoing damage).

### Charm
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 4th  
As *charm monster* (Will negates; duration 1 day/level).

### Curse of Despair
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 4th  
As *bestow curse* (Will negates). Choose one of the standard curse options.

### The Dead Walk
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 4th  
As *animate dead*. Animate undead up to twice the warlock's level in HD. The usual HD limit per casting applies.

### Fell Flight
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 4th | **Duration:** 24 hours  
Gain a **fly speed equal to land speed** (good maneuverability).

### flee the scene
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 4th  
As *dimension door*, plus leave behind a **major image** at the departure point (concentration).

### Hungry Darkness
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 4th | **Duration:** Concentration  
Create a **20-ft.-radius globe of darkness** (as *deeper darkness*). Bats swarm in the area; creatures in the area take 1d6 damage/round and must make Concentration checks to cast.

### Ignore the Pyre
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 4th | **Duration:** 24 hours  
Gain **resistance 10 to fire** (or improve existing fire resistance by 10, to a maximum of 30). Also, immunity to non-magical fire.

### Liquid Pain
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 4th  
As *inflict moderate wounds* (melee touch; 2d8+5 negative energy damage). Creates a small bottle of preserved pain as a byproduct — worth 25 gp as an alchemical reagent.

### See the Unseen (Lesser upgrade)
*(Warlocks who already have this as a Least invocation do not need to take it again — the Least version already provides this effect.)*

### Walk Unseen
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 4th | **Duration:** 24 hours  
As *invisibility* (continuous while active; breaks on attack as normal).

## Greater Invocations (Warlock 11+)

These can be selected starting at 11th level.

### Eldritch Line *(Blast Shape)*
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 6th  
Eldritch blast becomes a **120-ft. line** (Reflex half). All creatures in the area take damage.

### Noxious Blast *(Eldritch Essence)*
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 6th  
Target hit by eldritch blast must make a **Fort save** or become **nauseated** for 1 minute.

### Repelling Blast *(Eldritch Essence)*
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 6th  
Target hit by eldritch blast must make a **Fort save** or be knocked back 10 ft. and fall **prone**. Large or larger creatures may make a Str check (DC = damage dealt) to resist the knockback.

### Bewitching Blast *(Eldritch Essence)*
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 6th  
Target hit by eldritch blast must make a **Will save** or be **confused** for 1 round per 2 warlock levels.

### Chilling Tentacles
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 6th | **Duration:** Concentration  
As *black tentacles*, but the tentacles deal 2d6 cold damage per round in addition to grappling.

### Devour Magic
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 6th  
Touch attack; as *dispel magic* but more powerful. Gain **temporary hit points** equal to the spell level of each spell successfully dispelled, lasting 10 minutes (max 15 temporary HP).

### Enervating Shadow
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 6th | **Duration:** 24 hours  
Gain full **concealment** in any shadowy illumination (not darkness). Attackers in shade have a 20% miss chance against you.

### Hellish Rebuke
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 6th  
As *scorching ray* (ranged touch; multiple rays at higher levels) but deals **fire damage equal to eldritch blast damage** per ray. Can target a creature that has attacked you in the last round for +2d6 damage on one ray.

### Painful Slumber of Ages
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 6th  
As *flesh to stone* (Fort negates). Stone target does not decay; can be restored by *stone to flesh*.

### Path of Shadow
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 6th | **Duration:** Concentration  
Create a **shadow path** — teleport from one shadowed area to any other shadowed area you can see or know of (range unlimited). Travel is instantaneous.

### Steal Summoning
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 6th  
As *dimensional anchor*, but if the creature fails its Will save, you take control of it for 1 round/level (as if you had summoned it). After the duration, the creature vanishes (not returned to its original summoner).

### Tenacious Plague
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 6th | **Duration:** 24 hours  
As *insect plague* but the swarms follow a designated target and cannot be dispersed by normal means (require dispel magic or similar).

### Voracious Dispelling
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 6th  
As *dispel magic* (targeted or area). Any spell successfully dispelled causes the target to take **1d6 damage per spell level** dispelled (no save).

## Dark Invocations (Warlock 16+)

These can be selected starting at 16th level. Most reshape the eldritch blast in extraordinary ways or grant near-spell powers of extreme magnitude.

### Eldritch Doom *(Blast Shape)*
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 8th  
Eldritch blast becomes a **20-ft.-radius burst** centered on any point within range (Reflex half). All creatures in the area take full blast damage.

### Binding Blast *(Eldritch Essence)*
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 8th  
Target hit by eldritch blast must make a **Will save** or be **paralyzed** for 1 round.

### Dark Discorporation
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 8th | **Duration:** 24 hours  
Become a **swarm of Diminutive batlike creatures**; gain the swarm subtype, immunity to weapon damage, and can occupy any space a swarm can. Can still use invocations. Eldritch blast functions as a ranged touch attack from the swarm.

### Dark Foresight
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 8th | **Duration:** 24 hours  
As *foresight* (continuous; you cannot be surprised; +2 insight bonus to AC and Reflex saves; sense imminent danger). Additionally, share this foresight with one willing touched creature for the same duration.

### Energy Immunity
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 8th | **Duration:** 24 hours  
Choose one energy type (acid, cold, electricity, fire, or sonic). Gain **immunity** to that energy type for 24 hours. Replaces (does not stack with) any existing resistance to that energy type.

### Retributive Invisibility
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 8th | **Duration:** 24 hours  
As *greater invisibility* (continuous; does not break on attack). Additionally, creatures that successfully hit you while you are invisible take **1d6 damage per 2 warlock levels** (no save; untyped).

### Word of Changing
**Equiv. Spell Level:** 8th  
As *polymorph any object* (Fort negates for unwilling targets). Duration based on the target and degree of change, per standard polymorph any object rules.
