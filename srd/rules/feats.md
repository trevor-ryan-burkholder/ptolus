# Feats (Common Reference)

Feats every group hits during play. Not the full list — see PHB 89 for everything, *Complete* series for more.

## Combat staples

**Power Attack** (Str 13): Take up to your BAB as a penalty on attacks; gain equal bonus to damage. Two-handed weapon: bonus = 2× penalty. Prereq for many feats.

**Cleave** (Power Attack, Str 13): When you drop an opponent in melee, immediate free attack on adjacent foe at full BAB.

**Great Cleave** (Cleave, BAB +4): As Cleave, no limit on number per round.

**Combat Expertise** (Int 13): Take up to -5 attack, gain equal +AC for 1 round.

**Improved Trip / Disarm / Sunder** (Combat Expertise/Power Attack): No AoO for the attempt, +4 to the check.

**Combat Reflexes** (Dex 13): Additional AoOs/round equal to Dex mod. Can take AoOs while flat-footed.

**Dodge** (Dex 13): +1 dodge AC vs. one declared opponent. Prereq for Mobility, Spring Attack.

**Mobility** (Dodge): +4 AC against AoOs provoked by movement.

**Spring Attack** (Mobility, BAB +4): Move-attack-move (up to your speed) without provoking from the target.

**Weapon Focus** (BAB +1, proficient with weapon): +1 attack with one weapon. Prereq for Weapon Specialization (Fighter 4: +2 damage).

**Two-Weapon Fighting** (Dex 15): Reduces TWF penalties (-2/-2 instead of -4/-4 when off-hand light).

**Improved Initiative**: +4 initiative.

**Improved Critical** (Proficient, BAB +8): Double crit range for one weapon (does not stack with keen).

**Point Blank Shot**: +1 attack and damage with ranged attacks within 30 ft. Prereq for many archer feats.

**Precise Shot** (PBS): No -4 for shooting into melee.

**Rapid Shot** (PBS, Dex 13): Extra ranged attack/round; all attacks at -2.

**Manyshot** (Rapid Shot, BAB +6, Dex 17): Fire multiple arrows in one attack (each weaker but resolved on one attack roll).

## Caster staples

**Combat Casting**: +4 Concentration when casting defensively or grappled.

**Spell Focus** (school): +1 DC for saves vs. spells of chosen school. Greater Spell Focus: +1 more.

**Spell Penetration**: +2 caster level vs. spell resistance. Greater: +2 more.

**Empower Spell**: +2 spell slot level; all variable numeric effects ×1.5.

**Maximize Spell**: +3 spell slot level; all variable numeric effects maximized.

**Quicken Spell**: +4 spell slot level; cast as swift action (1 quickened spell/round).

**Still Spell**: +1 spell slot level; no somatic components (cast in armor without ASF).

**Silent Spell**: +1 spell slot level; no verbal components.

**Extend Spell**: +1 spell slot level; double duration.

**Heighten Spell**: cast at higher level slot (treat as that level for DC).

## Saves and defenses

**Great Fortitude / Lightning Reflexes / Iron Will**: +2 to the named save.

**Toughness**: +3 HP.

**Endurance**: +4 on Con checks vs. fatigue, suffocation, hot/cold; sleep in light/medium armor without penalty.

## Skill feats

**Skill Focus** (skill): +3 to that skill.

**Alertness**: +2 Listen and Spot.

**Stealthy**: +2 Hide and Move Silently.

**Persuasive**: +2 Bluff and Intimidate.

**Nimble Fingers**: +2 Disable Device and Open Lock.

**Self-Sufficient**: +2 Heal and Survival.

## Item creation (caster-side)

**Scribe Scroll** (CL 1+): wizards get free at 1st level. Cost: 12.5 × CL × spell level gp + 1 day per 1,000 gp; XP cost 1/25 of price.

**Brew Potion** (CL 3+): 50 × CL × spell level gp; XP cost 1/25 of price. Limit: 3rd-level spells.

**Craft Wand** (CL 5+): 750 × CL × spell level gp; max 4th-level spells; 50 charges.

**Craft Wondrous Item** (CL 3+): ½ market price in gp and XP cost 1/25 of price.

**Craft Magic Arms and Armor** (CL 5+): ½ market price.

## Metamagic note

All metamagic feats raise the spell's effective slot level. A spontaneous caster (sorcerer, bard) takes a full-round action to cast a metamagic'd spell.
