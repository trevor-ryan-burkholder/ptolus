## Common DCs

- 10 -- easy
- 15 -- moderate
- 20 -- challenging
- 25 -- hard, requires training
- 30 -- very hard
- 40+ -- heroic

## Per-Skill Quick Reference

### Appraise (Int, untrained)
Value item: DC 12 common, DC 15 masterwork/exotic. Fail by 5+ = wildly wrong estimate. Synergy: Craft (same type) +2.

### Balance (Dex, untrained, ACP)
Walk narrow surface: 12 in. wide DC 5, 6 in. DC 10, 4 in. DC 15, 2 in. DC 20, 1 in. DC 25. Slippery +5. Running +5. Synergy: Tumble +2.

### Bluff (Cha, untrained)
Opposed by Sense Motive. Target is suspicious +5 DC; outrageous lie +10-20. Combat feint: DC = target's Sense Motive; success denies Dex to AC on your next melee attack that round.

### Climb (Str, untrained, ACP)
Move at 1/4 speed (1/2 at -5). Fail by 5+ = fall from current height.

| Surface | DC |
|---|---|
| Very rough wall, knotted rope | 5 |
| Natural rock face | 10 |
| Uneven stone wall | 15 |
| Smooth stone, unknotted rope | 20 |
| Slippery wall | 25 |
| Overhang/ceiling with handholds | 20 |

Synergy: Use Rope +2 with knotted rope.

### Concentration (Con, untrained)
Cast while disturbed. Fail = spell lost (action and slot wasted).

| Distraction | DC |
|---|---|
| Damage during casting | 10 + damage + spell level |
| Continuous damage (on fire) | 10 + 1/2 damage/rd + spell level |
| Vigorous motion (rough sea) | 10 + spell level |
| Violent motion (quake, charge) | 15 + spell level |
| Entangled | 15 |
| Grappling or pinned | 20 + spell level |
| Weather | 5 |
| Cast defensively (avoid AoO) | 15 + spell level |

### Craft (Int, untrained)
DC x price in sp = weekly progress result. Common DCs: simple weapons 12, cloth/leather 10, armor 12-18, masterwork +2.

### Decipher Script (Int, trained only)
1 page, 1 minute: DC 20 simple, 25 standard, 30+ obscure. Fail by 5+ = misread.

### Diplomacy (Cha, untrained)
Shift attitude one step (hostile-unfriendly-indifferent-friendly-helpful). Takes 1 minute for negotiation; single-round modifier possible at +5 DC.

| Starting attitude | DC (one step better) |
|---|---|
| Hostile | 25 |
| Unfriendly | 20 |
| Indifferent | 15 |
| Friendly | 10 |

Synergy: Bluff +2, Knowledge (nobility) +2, Sense Motive +2.

### Disable Device (Int, trained only)
Requires thieves' tools (-2 without). Masterwork tools +2.

| Device | DC |
|---|---|
| Simple lock | 20 |
| Average lock | 25 |
| Good lock | 30 |
| Superior lock | 40 |
| Simple trap | 20 |
| Tricky trap | 25 |
| Difficult trap | 30 |
| Fiendish trap | 40 |
| Magic trap | 25-30 |

### Disguise (Cha, untrained)
-2 per difference category (gender, race, age, impersonating specific person). Synergy: Bluff +2 when in disguise and performing. Opposed by Spot (-1 per 3 ranks in Disguise).

### Escape Artist (Dex, untrained, ACP)
Ropes: vs binder's Use Rope check +10. Manacles: DC 30. Superior manacles: DC 35. Net: DC 20. Tight space: DC 30.

### Forgery (Int, untrained)
Time: 1 min/page. Quality of tools matters. Opposed by Int check of reader (not a skill check -- but familiarity helps). Languages/scripts required to forge in them.

### Gather Information (Cha, untrained)
1d4+1 hours, spending at least 1d4x5 cp on drinks/bribes: DC 10 for common knowledge, 15 for less known, 20 for obscure, 25 for secrets. In Ptolus, use the city's rumor system first.

### Handle Animal (Cha, trained only)
Teach trick: DC 15, 1 week. Push animal (untrained): DC 25. Handle in combat: DC 10. Rear wild: DC 15+HD. Synergy: Wild empathy (druid, ranger) uses this.

### Heal (Wis, untrained)
Stabilize dying: DC 15, standard action. First aid (+1d4 hp, 1 minute): DC 10. Long-term care: DC 15, bed rest, heals 2x normal rate. Treat disease/poison: DC = disease/poison DC; grants +4 on target's next save. Healer's kit +2 circumstance.

### Hide (Dex, untrained, ACP)
Opposed by Spot. Need cover or concealment to hide. Running: -10. Sniping from hiding: DC 20 to re-hide after ranged attack (-20 if attack was melee). Synergy: Disguise +2 using makeup for camouflage.

### Intimidate (Cha, untrained)
Demoralize in combat: vs target level + Wis mod; succeed = shaken 1 round. Long-term (1 minute out of combat): forced cooperation for 1d6x10 min.

### Jump (Str, untrained, ACP)
Running start (10 ft. min) needed for most jumps. Without: x4 DC.

| Jump | DC |
|---|---|
| Long jump 10 ft. | 10 |
| Long jump 20 ft. | 20 |
| High jump 1 ft. | 4 |
| High jump 3 ft. | 12 |
| High jump 5 ft. | 20 |

Synergy: Tumble +2. Speed bonus: +4 to Jump per 10 ft. above 30 ft.

### Knowledge (Int, trained only)
Recall lore: DC 10 common, 15 uncommon, 20 obscure. Identify monster: DC 10+CR (recognise it); +5 = learn one special power/ability. Sub-skills: arcana, dungeoneering, geography, history, local, nature, nobility/royalty, religion, the planes.

### Listen (Wis, untrained)
+1 DC per 10 ft. distance. -4 per closed door; -8 per wall. Hear creature: DC 0 (normal) to 10+ (careful creature). Opposed by Move Silently.

### Move Silently (Dex, untrained, ACP)
Opposed by Listen. Running: -10. Metal armor: ACP applies. Soft ground: +2 bonus to Move Silently.

### Open Lock (Dex, trained only)
Requires thieves' tools (-2 without). See Disable Device table for lock DCs. Time: 1 round.

### Perform (Cha, untrained)
Earn money: DC 10 = 1d10 cp/day, DC 15 = 1d10 sp, DC 20 = 3d10 sp, DC 25 = 1d6 gp. Bard performances use this for class features (no check needed for inspire courage etc.).

### Profession (Wis, trained only)
Earn half check result in gp per week. Mostly background flavor.

### Ride (Dex, untrained)
See mounted-combat.md. Key DCs: stay in saddle DC 5; fight with war-trained mount DC 10; soft fall DC 15; fast mount DC 20. Synergy: Handle Animal +2.

### Search (Int, untrained)
Find concealed door: DC 20 (secret). Find hidden object: DC varies. Detect magical trap: requires Disable Device training (DC 25-30). 1 minute per 5-ft. square to thoroughly search. Synergy: Survival +2 to find tracks.

### Sense Motive (Wis, untrained)
Detect lie: opposed vs Bluff. Hunch (something wrong): DC 20. Discern hidden motive: DC 25. Passive -- can always try.

### Sleight of Hand (Dex, trained only, ACP)
Palm small object: DC 10 when not observed; opposed by Spot in combat. Pick pocket: DC 20 (+5 if aware). Conceal weapon: DC 20 (opponent's Spot -4). Without training: -5 penalty.

### Spellcraft (Int, trained only)
Identify spell being cast: DC 15+spell level. Identify from effect: DC 20+spell level. Identify potion (detect magic + taste): DC 25. Learn from scroll: DC 15+spell level. Synergy: Knowledge (arcana) +2.

### Spot (Wis, untrained)
+1 DC per 10 ft. -4 distraction. Opposed by Hide. Passive -- DM calls for it. Notice creature in dim light: -4. Very large creature: +4 bonus.

### Survival (Wis, untrained)
Survive in wild: DC 10 (temperate), 15 (desert), 20 (arctic). Track: DC 10 (soft ground/clear trail) to 30+ (hard surface/days old). Avoid hazards: DC 15. Synergy: Knowledge (nature) +2, Handle Animal +2.

### Swim (Str, untrained, ACP x2)
Calm: DC 10; rough: DC 15; stormy: DC 20. Fail by 5+ = underwater (drowning rules). Armor check penalty applies twice. Synergy: 5+ Swim ranks = +8 on drowning checks.

### Tumble (Dex, trained only, ACP)
Move through threatened square without AoO: DC 15 (half speed). Move through enemy square: DC 25. Fall safely (reduce 1d6 per 10 ranks): DC 15 per 10 ft. Synergy: Jump +2, Balance +2.

### Use Magic Device (Cha, trained only)
Emulate class feature: DC 20. Emulate ability score: DC 15. Emulate race: DC 25. Use scroll (not on spell list): DC 20+CL. Activate blindly: DC 25. Fail by 10+ = miscasting curse. Synergy: Spellcraft +2, Knowledge (arcana) +2.

### Use Rope (Dex, untrained)
Tie simple knot: DC 10. Secure someone: DC 15 (target Escape Artist vs your check +10). Splice: DC 15. Throw grappling hook: DC 10 (close) to 20+ (far). Synergy: Climb +2 using rope you set up, Escape Artist +2 to escape own bonds.

*Sources: PHB 63-102.*
