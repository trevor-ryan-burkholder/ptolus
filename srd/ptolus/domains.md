# New Domains — Complete Divine & Defenders of the Faith

Domains introduced in **Complete Divine** (CD) and **Defenders of the Faith** (DotF).
These supplement the core 3.5e domains in the PHB.

DotF domains are labeled **Prestige Domains** — they require a prestige class feature
(typically a 3rd domain granted by a divine prestige class such as Church Inquisitor,
Divine Oracle, or Contemplative) rather than being available at cleric 1st level.

**Ptolus note:** The Church of Lothian's core domains are Law, Good, Sun, Protection,
and Healing. Domains below that expand those or are relevant to the Ptolus pantheon
are called out in each entry.

## Defenders of the Faith — Prestige Domains

Prestige domains are a 3rd domain granted by certain divine prestige classes (see `prestige-classes.md`). They are **not available to standard clerics at 1st level.** Each prestige domain entry in DotF lists which prestige classes can grant it.

Note: DotF is a 3.0 sourcebook. The prestige domain mechanical text uses 3.0 conventions (e.g., +1d4 to stats for cat's grace, "partial action" for haste). Use the 3.5e equivalents when playing.
