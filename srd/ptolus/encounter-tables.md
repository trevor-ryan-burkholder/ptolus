# Ptolus Random Encounter Tables

Source: *Ptolus: City by the Spire* — Random Encounter Matrix (Appendix/GM Screen, pp. 585–586 print; GM screen reference sheets).

---

## How the System Works

The Ptolus encounter system uses **numbered encounter descriptions** (1–317) rather than direct creature stat blocks. The d% matrix maps dice rolls to encounter numbers by district. Some entries have two numbers separated by a slash (`Day/Night`) — roll or choose based on time of day.

**Usage notes (per the book):**
- This matrix isn't meant to generate every creature the PCs pass by. It generates *potential encounters* — many are flavor, some are plot hooks, few are combat.
- Good rule of thumb: one street encounter per session, or one every other session.
- Ignore results that make no sense in context.
- Don't overuse any single encounter — change details if running it twice.

### City Streets (use the matrix above by district)

The matrix covers 11 surface districts. For quick reference, encounter **tone by district**:

| District | Character of Encounters |
|----------|------------------------|
| Nobles' Quarter | Aristocrats, house servants, Knights of the Pale; very few criminals or monsters |
| Midtown | Mixed — adventurers, merchants, City Watch, occasional weird event |

| South Market | Craftspeople, guilders, labor disputes, merchants |
| North Market | Vendors, farmers, travelers arriving/departing, petty crime |

| Rivergate | Commoners, watchmen, constables; quiet at night |

| Warrens | Criminals, monsters (Grade 3–4), gangs; extremely dangerous at night |
| Necropolis | Clerics, gravediggers by day; undead and cultists at night |

### Necropolis (canonical)

Use the **Necropolis column** of the main d% matrix. Per the book:
- For the **Dark Reliquary** specifically, there is a 10% chance per move of an encounter. Use Necropolis column, *Night results only* (number after the slash).
- Day results weight toward clerics, gravediggers, and pilgrims.
- Night results weight heavily toward undead, cultists, and demons.

Notable nighttime Necropolis encounters:
- **253**: 1d3 ghouls in shadows (CR 1 per)
- **254**: Vampire spawn waiting in ambush (CR 2)
- **256**: Vampire (fighter level 1d12; CR 8–13 range)
- **292**: 1d6 ghouls in ambush (CR 1 per)
- **313**: 2d4 shadows (CR 3 per)
- **314**: 2d6 zombies (CR 1/2 per)
- **315**: Forsaken cultists + cleric performing a ritual
- **316**: Forsaken cultists among gravestones
- **317**: Cultists seeking a kidnap victim
