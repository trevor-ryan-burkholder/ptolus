# Epic Level Handbook Prestige Classes
**Source:** Epic Level Handbook (ELH) Chapter 1, pp. 24–36

Epic prestige classes require character level 21+ to enter. They do not grant base attack or save bonus increases; use the epic BAB/save table. These are "new" classes entered via multiclassing at epic levels.

---

### Agent Retriever
- **Source:** ELH p. 24
- **Requirements:** Any lawful; Gather Information 24 ranks, Knowledge (planes) 15 ranks; Track feat
- **HD:** d6 | **BAB:** Epic | **Saves:** Epic
- **Skill Points:** 6 + Int/level
- **Key features:**
  - Uncanny Location (Sp): Spend 1 day attuning to a target; thereafter automatically knows its location as *discern location* as long as actively pursuing it
  - Plane Shift (Sp): At 2nd level, 1/day; +1 use every 5 levels
  - Force Sphere (Sp): At 3rd level — enclose a visible creature within 30 ft. in a force sphere (Reflex DC 20+) for up to 7 days; 1/day, +1 use every 5 levels
  - Ethereal Jaunt (Sp): At 4th level, 1/day; +1 use every 5 levels
  - Tracking Bonus (Ex): +10 insight to Wilderness Lore checks to track; +10 every 5 levels
  - Spells per day: +1 level of existing spellcasting class at each level
  - Bonus feat every 5 levels (5, 10, 15, 20...)
- **Notes:** Best for rangers/rogues/bards who need interplanar hunting capability. Essentially a planar bounty hunter.

### Cosmic Descryer
- **Source:** ELH p. 26
- **Requirements:** Knowledge (planes) 24 ranks; Spell Focus (Conjuration); Epic feat: Energy Resistance; ability to cast *gate* and any planar ally/binding spell; must have traveled to another plane
- **HD:** d4 | **BAB:** Epic | **Saves:** Epic
- **Skill Points:** 2 + Int/level
- **Key features:**
  - Superior Planar Summoning (Ex): Starting at 1st level, can increase summoning spells (elemental swarm, gate, greater planar ally/binding, summon monster IX, etc.) to affect creatures 4 HD higher than normal; +4 HD every 4 levels
  - Naturalization (Ex): At 2nd level, gains resistance to planar effects for one plane she has visited; +1 plane every 2 levels
  - Enduring Gate (Su): At 3rd level, can make a *gate* spell last 1 full day rather than 1 round/level; +1 day every 3 levels
  - Cosmic Connection (Su): At 7th level, merge with multiverse energy 1/day for minutes equal to class level; while connected: immune to critical hits, native on any plane, *dimension door* at will (20th-level caster), and can boost attack rolls/saves/checks/caster level at cost of 5 hp per +1 bonus
  - Spells per day: +1 level of existing class every other cosmic descryer level
  - Bonus feat at 5th and every 5 levels
- **Notes:** Pure spellcaster epic PrC. Outstanding for planar-focused campaigns; dramatically expands planar summoning and gate ability.

### Divine Emissary
- **Source:** ELH p. 27
- **Requirements:** BAB +23; Knowledge (religion) 10 ranks; Weapon Focus (deity's favored weapon); Epic feat: Great Smiting; must have patron deity; must complete a deity-impressing quest (DM choice, not player)
- **HD:** d10 | **BAB:** Epic | **Saves:** Epic
- **Skill Points:** 4 + Int/level
- **Key features:**
  - Granted Domain (Ex): Access to one of deity's domains plus its granted power
  - Divine Inspiration (Sp): At 1st level, +2 luck bonus on attacks and damage for 10 rounds, 1/day; +1 use every 3 levels
  - Extra Smite (Su): +2 smite attempts/day at 2nd level, +1 every 3 levels
  - Greater Planar Ally (Sp): At 3rd level, 1/day (no return favor required); +1 use every 10 levels
  - Divine Hand (Su): At 9th level, +20 sacred bonus on next attack roll with deity's favored weapon as a free action; 1/day, +1 every 10 levels
  - Spells per day: +1 level of existing class at each level
  - Bonus feat at 6th and every 10 levels (very slow)
- **Notes:** Strong for combat-focused divine casters (paladin/cleric). The deity-quest requirement makes entry controlled by the DM.

### Epic Infiltrator
- **Source:** ELH p. 28
- **Requirements:** Any nonchaotic; Bluff 24 ranks, Disguise 24 ranks, Diplomacy 10 ranks, Read Lips 10 ranks; Alertness; Epic feat: Polyglot; must have spent 1 month disguised as someone else
- **HD:** d6 | **BAB:** Epic | **Saves:** Epic
- **Skill Points:** 8 + Int/level
- **Key features:**
  - Improved Cover Identity (Ex/Su): 3 cover identities at 1st level; +1 every 4 levels. Each grants +4 Disguise/+2 Bluff/+2 Gather Info circumstance bonuses. Always under *undetectable alignment*
  - Sneak Attack: +1d6 at 1st level, +1d6 every 3 levels
  - Specialist Training (Ex): At 2nd level and every 3 levels, +3 bonus to a chosen skill group (Concealment, Subterfuge, Espionage, or Interaction)
  - Read Thoughts (Su): At 3rd level, 1/day *detect thoughts* on one target; +1 use every 4 levels
  - Mind Blank (Sp): At 3rd level, 1/day; +1 use every 8 levels
  - Far Senses (Su): At 4th level, 1/day; extend vision or hearing to a known location within (20 ft. + 20 ft./level); +1 use every 2 levels
- **Notes:** The definitive espionage class. Excellent for rogue/bard builds. The cover identity + alignment-undetectable combination is extremely potent.

### Guardian Paramount
- **Source:** ELH p. 30
- **Requirements:** BAB +15; Spot 13 ranks; Alertness, Lightning Reflexes; Epic feats: Blinding Speed, Superior Initiative; must have uncanny dodge and evasion
- **HD:** d10 | **BAB:** Epic | **Saves:** Epic
- **Skill Points:** 4 + Int/level
- **Key features:**
  - Uncanny Dodge Enabler (Ex): Extend own uncanny dodge benefits to one designated creature within 5 ft.; 3/day at 1st level, +1 every 3 levels
  - Evasive Preceptor (Ex): Extend own evasion/improved evasion to one designated creature within 5 ft.; 1/day at 2nd, +1 every 3 levels
  - Protective Aura (Sp): Modified *shield other* that transfers wounds as subdual damage; 1/day at 3rd, +1 every 3 levels
  - Adjust Probability (Ex): Force a reroll of any attack/check/save within 25 ft. of any creature (friend or foe); 2/day at 5th, +1 every 3 levels
  - Call Back (Sp): At 6th level, cast *true resurrection* (20th-level caster) on a creature previously aided; 1/day, +1 every 6 levels
  - Bonus feat at 1st level and every 3 levels (1, 4, 7, 10...)
- **Notes:** Outstanding bodyguard PrC. Adjust Probability is extremely potent — forcing rerolls of enemy successes. Best for rogue/fighter/cleric builds.

### High Proselytizer
- **Source:** ELH p. 31
- **Requirements:** Diplomacy 12 ranks; Knowledge (religion) or Knowledge (nature) 24 ranks; Leadership; Epic feat: Epic Leadership; must cast 5th-level divine spells; must have patron deity
- **HD:** d8 | **BAB:** Epic | **Saves:** Epic
- **Skill Points:** 2 + Int/level
- **Key features:**
  - Proselytize (Sp): Powerful mass-compulsion preaching ability — enraptured listeners act under *charm person*; low-HD followers auto-fail save; gains additional effects (deific touch, word, face, aura) at 3rd/5th/7th/9th levels respectively
  - Heal (Sp): 1/day at 2nd level, +1 every 4 levels
  - Spells per day: +1 level of existing divine class every other level
  - Bonus feat every 4 levels (4, 8, 12...)
- **Notes:** Very narrow use case — dominates crowds but weak in dungeon contexts. Strong in political/urban campaigns like Ptolus where factions and cult activity are central.

### Legendary Dreadnought
- **Source:** ELH p. 33
- **Requirements:** BAB +23; Intimidate 15 ranks; Combat Reflexes, Great Cleave, Improved Bull Rush, Improved Critical (any weapon)
- **HD:** d12 | **BAB:** Epic | **Saves:** Epic
- **Skill Points:** 2 + Int/level
- **Key features:**
  - Unstoppable (Ex): +20 bonus on Strength checks to break/burst objects or walls of force, OR +20 on one attack roll; 1/day at 1st, +1 every 5 levels
  - Unmovable (Ex): +20 bonus to resist grapple, bull rush, trip, or forced movement — or grants a Will save against effects that don't normally allow one; 1/day at 2nd, +1 every 5 levels
  - Shrug Off Punishment (Ex): +12 bonus hit points at 3rd level, +12 more every 5 levels
  - Thick Skinned (Ex): DR 3/– at 4th level, +3 every 5 levels; stacks with class/feat-based DR but not magic items
  - Bonus feat at 5th and every 5 levels
- **Notes:** Pure tank. No spellcasting, no skill tricks — just escalating durability and combat dominance. Best for barbarian/fighter builds hitting BAB +23.

### Perfect Wight
- **Source:** ELH p. 34
- **Requirements:** Hide 24 ranks, Move Silently 24 ranks; Epic feat: Self-Concealment; sneak attack +10d6
- **HD:** d6 | **BAB:** Epic | **Saves:** Epic
- **Skill Points:** 8 + Int/level
- **Key features:**
  - Improved Invisibility (Su): As the spell (20th-level caster); 1/day at 1st, +1 every 5 levels
  - Improved Legerdemain (Su): Perform Disable Device, Open Lock, Pick Pocket, or Search at 30 ft. range; or make one melee sneak/death attack at 30 ft. range (as if flanking); 1/day at 2nd, +1 every 5 levels
  - Incorporeal (Su): Become incorporeal for 20 + class level rounds; 1/day at 3rd, +1 every 5 levels
  - Shadow Form (Su): At 4th level — incorporeal + immune to crits + fly 100 ft. (good); can boost any roll at cost of 7 hp per +1; 1/day, +1 every 5 levels
  - Bonus feat at 5th and every 5 levels
- **Notes:** The most powerful stealth-focused PrC in the game. Shadow Form at epic levels makes this character functionally unkillable by mundane means. Rogue/assassin build.

### Union Sentinel
- **Source:** ELH p. 35
- **Requirements:** Any lawful; BAB +21; Diplomacy 8 ranks, Knowledge (local) 3 ranks; Alertness, Improved Disarm; Epic feat: Armor Skin; must reside in the demiplane-city of Union
- **HD:** d10 | **BAB:** Epic | **Saves:** Epic
- **Skill Points:** 2 + Int/level
- **Key features (all spell-like abilities):**
  - Sending 1/day (1st level), +1 every 7 levels
  - Shield of Law 1/day (1st level), +1 every 3 levels
  - Freedom 1/day (2nd level), +1 every 6 levels
  - Knock 1/day (2nd level), +1 every 4 levels
  - Dimensional Anchor 1/day (3rd level), +1 every 3 levels
  - Portal Guardian (Su): Block any portal/gate within 5 ft. from being activated; 1/day at 4th, +1 every 6 levels
  - Forcecage 1/day (5th level), +1 every 5 levels
  - Imprisonment 1/week (7th level), +1 every 5 levels
- **Notes:** The "must reside in Union" requirement is setting-specific but can be reflavored for a Ptolus campaign (e.g., a knight of a particular order headquartered in a specific location). Excellent for a lawful fighter/cleric who specializes in containment and planar threat response.

| Agent Retriever | Ranger/Rogue | Tracker/bounty hunter | Optional |
| Cosmic Descryer | Wizard/Cleric | Planar summoner | Required |
| Divine Emissary | Paladin/Cleric | Combat divine | Required |
| Epic Infiltrator | Rogue/Bard | Spy/infiltrator | Optional |
| Guardian Paramount | Rogue/Fighter | Bodyguard | No |
| High Proselytizer | Cleric | Mass-preaching | Required (divine) |
| Legendary Dreadnought | Fighter/Barbarian | Tank | No |
| Perfect Wight | Rogue/Assassin | Ultimate stealth | No |
| Union Sentinel | Fighter/Cleric | Lawful enforcer | No |
