## Long Arcs (Background / Dormant)

### Karn's Fate
**Status:** Dormant — do not surface until level 20+
Karn departed for the Spire at campaign start. His old party is likely somewhere inside Jabel Shammar. Whatever happened on his first expedition shaped him. The PCs will eventually find out.

### The Banewarrens / Jabel Shammar Seal
**Status:** Dormant — epic tier only
The Banewarrens are fused into Jabel Shammar. Evil artifacts drift toward the fortress. The seal is under constant pressure. This is the structural endgame of the campaign.

### Orcus in the Fortress
**Status:** Dormant — epic tier only
Orcus is imprisoned within Jabel Shammar as part of the seal architecture. He is not the villain so much as a load-bearing problem. The final arc will force a choice about what to do with him.

### The Covenant of the Third Thought
**Status:** Background — ongoing
The Covenant is operating beneath the city at all times. They know about the PCs before the PCs know about them. Their goals align with the party's survival but their methods are alien. First meaningful contact likely Arc III+.
