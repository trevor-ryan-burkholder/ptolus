# Kevris Killraven

**Role:** Crime lord; founder of the Killraven Crime League (see [[../factions/the-killravens]]).
**Seat:** Killraven's Tower, North Market. Arrived in Ptolus 720 IA.

## Stat block
Unstatted — a major recurring antagonist. Rumored to be more than human; keep her capabilities ambiguous until it matters.

## What she knows
- The weak points of the Balacazar empire she is dismantling.
- Whatever her true backers know — a deliberate mystery.

## What she wants
- A criminal empire of her own, taken from the Balacazars by force and cunning.
- Something specific her organization is built to acquire (define as the arc develops).

## Relationships
- **At war with:** the [[../factions/balacazars]].
- Hires deniable outside talent (a recurring source of jobs for the party).

## Notes
Her arrival is one year old as the campaign opens; the Scarlet Death outbreak of 720 IA is recent memory. Her true nature is a thread worth seeding early and paying off late. *(Ptolus p. 315.)*
