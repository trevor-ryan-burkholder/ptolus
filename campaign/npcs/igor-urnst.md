# Commissar Igor Urnst

**Race/Class:** Human, Fighter 18 (a former Imperial general).
**Role:** The Commissar — Imperial governor of Ptolus, chair of the City Council, commander of the City Watch and the Commissar's Men, and master of the Imperial Eyes.
**Seat:** Dalenguard, Oldtown.

## Stat block
Unstatted here — Fighter 18; treat as a named, formidable NPC only if combat is unavoidable (it almost never should be).

## What he knows
- Effectively everything the Imperial Eyes overhear — which is a great deal. He watches the noble houses, the guilds, and the factions.
- Which laws are being bent and by whom; he tolerates much in the name of order.

## What he wants
- **Order**, above justice. A stable, governable Ptolus that keeps the Empire's confidence while three emperors squabble over the throne.
- No faction — criminal, arcane, or chaos — strong enough to threaten his control.

## Relationships
- Commands the City Watch and the Commissar's Men; runs the Imperial Eyes.
- Wary of the Inverted Pyramid (which sits above his authority) and the noble houses.
- Has no love for the crime families but uses them when convenient.

## Notes
The party will feel his reach long before they meet him. Crossing the Eyes is dangerous; earning his quiet approval opens doors. *(Ptolus p. 320.)*
