# Karn

**Race/Class/Level:** Human Eldritch Knight 25 (3.5e optimized gish build)
**Role:** Former mentor to the PCs. Veteran delver. Survivor of a failed apex expedition.
**Status:** Departed for the Spire at campaign start. Whereabouts unknown. *(Dormant until level 20+.)*

---

## Identity

Karn is the most accomplished delver the PCs have ever known — a 3.5e-optimized gish who combines arcane firepower with martial competence at a level few reach. He trained the party under the team name **"The Adventurers."** He is emotionally scarred from whatever happened on his first Spire expedition but remains highly competent and clear-eyed about danger.

He is not warm. He is reliable.

## What He Did

At campaign start, Karn:
- Left a letter for the PCs
- Handed them his home, his business, and his contacts
- Departed alone for the Spire

No explanation beyond the letter.

## What He Knows

Karn knows more about Jabel Shammar than anyone the PCs will meet before level 18. What happened on his first expedition is unknown. His old party is likely still inside the fortress.

## What He Wants

Unknown. He went back. That tells you something.

## Relationships

- **The PCs** — trained them, trusts them enough to leave everything in their hands.

## Stat Block

*Unstatted in detail — epic-tier NPC, not expected to appear until level 20+. When needed, build as EK 25 with Arcane Strike, Practiced Spellcaster, and Mithral Full Plate. Treat as CR 20 encounter if combat becomes necessary.*
