# Lord Zavere

**Role:** Lord of Castle Shard (with Lady Rill), one of the great benevolent powers of Ptolus.
**Seat:** Castle Shard, Nobles' Quarter (see [[../locations/castle-shard]]).

## Stat block
Unstatted — a high-level, resourceful figure. Peaceful in nearly all dealings with worthy adventurers.

## What he knows
- A great deal about the Spire, the Banewarrens, the chaos cults, and the city's hidden history.
- Who among the nobles and factions can be trusted — and who cannot (he opposes House Vladaam and the cults).

## What he wants
- To protect Ptolus from the rising chaos without ruling it.
- Capable, trustworthy agents to act where Castle Shard cannot openly move.

## Relationships
- Partner: **Lady Rill**. Served by a capable household (seneschal, guards, agents).
- **Opposes:** House Vladaam, the [[../factions/the-forsaken]], the Cults of Chaos.
- A potential patron and the party's most powerful good-aligned ally.

## Notes
Being accepted as agents — or even lords — of Castle Shard is a campaign-level achievement and a major plot engine. *(Ptolus p. 285.)*
