# The Balacazars

**Type:** Crime family (the dominant organized-crime power in Ptolus)
**Seat:** Holdings across the Warrens, Docks, and South Market; legitimate fronts citywide.

## Goals
- Maintain their grip on Ptolus's underworld — extortion, smuggling, fencing, gambling, contraband.
- Crush or absorb the upstart Killravens before they take real territory.
- Keep useful nobles and Watch officers bought; stay one step ahead of the Sisterhood of Silence.

## Current activities
- Quietly buying property and debt (warehouses on the Docks, leases in the Warrens).
- Moving chaositech and prohibited goods through the harbor — a lucrative, dangerous new line.
- Calling in debts to fund a war footing against the Killravens.

## Leadership
- **Menon Balacazar** — aged patriarch; cautious, calculating, rarely seen.
- **Malkeen Balacazar** — ambitious son and second-in-command; employs elite bodyguards implanted with chaositech by the Surgeon in the Shadows (Ptolus p. 571).
- **Fesamere & Maystra Balacazar** — Menon's two daughters. Maystra has ambitions of her own that don't match her father's plan for Malkeen to inherit.

## Disposition toward the party
*Starting: neutral/unaware.* They notice anyone who disrupts their operations or aids the Killravens. They prefer to buy or intimidate before they kill.

## Relationships
- **Rivals:** the [[the-killravens]] (open conflict brewing).
- **Quiet ties:** House Vladaam; assorted bought Watch officers.
- **Enemies:** the Sisterhood of Silence; honest elements of the City Watch.
