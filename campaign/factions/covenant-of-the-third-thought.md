# The Covenant of the Third Thought

**Type:** Hidden psionic shadow faction
**Alignment:** Neutral (not evil)
**Power Level:** Likely the most powerful hidden faction in the world
**Base of Operations:** Beneath the city — exact location unknown

---

## Core Belief

*"If Ptolus falls, the world follows."*

The Covenant exists to prevent that outcome by any means necessary, operating from a position of perfect information and near-invisible influence.

---

## What They Are

Inspired by: Bene Gesserit, Mentats, Illithid logic, Aboleth memory, drow conspiracy culture.

The Covenant is the **hidden maintenance crew of civilization** — a psionic intelligence network that has been stabilizing Ptolus from the shadows for longer than most institutions have existed. They do not seek power for its own sake. They seek continuity.

---

## What They Do

- Monitor all existential threats to the city (the Banewarrens, the Spire, Special Grade incidents, faction destabilization)
- Manipulate politics subtly — nudging outcomes rather than dictating them
- Cooperate with illithids and aboleths when interests align
- Maintain back-channels to most major factions without those factions knowing they exist
- Intervene directly only when the city faces collapse

---

## Leadership

Unknown. The Covenant does not present a face.

---

## Disposition Toward the Party

Unknown — monitoring. If the PCs become significant enough, the Covenant will be aware of them long before the reverse is true.

---

## Faction Notes

- First introduction likely Arc III or later (level 11+)
- Their cooperation with aboleths and illithids makes them unsettling even when helpful — their ends are good, their methods are alien
- They know about Orcus in Jabel Shammar. Whether they view this as a feature or a problem is unclear.
