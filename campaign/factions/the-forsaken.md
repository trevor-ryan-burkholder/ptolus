# The Forsaken

**Type:** Demon-worshipping cult (mortal servants of chaos)
**Seat:** The Dark Reliquary, in the Necropolis (see [[../locations/dark-reliquary]]).

## Goals
- Bring about the **Night of Dissolution** — the awakening of the Galchutt, the sleeping Lords of Chaos beneath Ptolus.
- Open the Banewarrens and loose what the Dread One sealed away.
- Spread chaositech and unite the scattered Cults of Chaos under one purpose.

## Current activities
- Rituals in the Necropolis after dark; kidnappings for sacrifice; quiet recruitment among the desperate and the noble alike.
- Working through cult cells (the Ebon Hand, the Rat God cult, and others) seeded across the city.
- Mapping the Banewarrens' outer seals — and getting close.

## Leadership
- The **Fallen** — actual demons, including the demon lords **Raguel and Lilith**, who direct the mortal Forsaken from the Dark Reliquary.
- Mortal cult leaders run individual cells; the Wintersouled undead serve as muscle.

## Disposition toward the party
*Starting: hostile (once aware).* The Forsaken are the campaign's central antagonists. Early on they are a rumor; by Arc III+ they are an open threat.

## Relationships
- **Opposed by:** the [[knights-of-the-pale]], the Keepers of the Veil, the Sisterhood of Silence, the Church of Lothian.
- **Tied to:** the Cults of Chaos citywide; the Banewarrens.

## Notes
Drives the City Turn "Awaken the Galchutt" and "Open the Banewarrens" clocks. *(Ptolus p. 232; Night of Dissolution.)*
