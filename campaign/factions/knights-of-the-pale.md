# The Knights of the Pale

**Type:** Militant holy order (paladins and holy warriors of Lothian)
**Seat:** Temple District (close ties to the Cathedral of St. Valien).

## Goals
- Hunt and destroy evil in Ptolus — especially undead, demons, and the chaos cults.
- Defend the Church of Lothian and the city's faithful.
- Keep the Necropolis's horrors contained behind its walls.

## Current activities
- Responding to Forsaken sightings near the Temple District and incursions from the Necropolis.
- Escorting clergy and warding holy sites on high holy days (Watch coverage thins then; crime rises elsewhere).
- Quietly investigating chaositech and cult contraband surfacing in the markets.

## Leadership
- **Captain Dierna Hillerchaun** leads the Knights of the Pale; she was recently named to fill an empty seat among the Commissar's Twelve Commanders. *(Ptolus p. 125.)*
- **Sir Beck** is a notable senior knight (lives on High Road, Oldtown) — a useful contact for higher-level parties.

## Disposition toward the party
*Starting: friendly-but-watchful.* Natural allies against the Forsaken; they expect good conduct and have little patience for necromancy, chaositech, or cutting corners.

## Relationships
- **Allied (uneasily):** the Church of Lothian; the Keepers of the Veil; the Sisterhood of Silence.
- **Enemies:** the [[the-forsaken]], the Fallen, the Cults of Chaos, intelligent undead.

## Notes
A good patron faction for righteous parties and a moral foil for greyer ones. *(Ptolus, factions chapter.)*
