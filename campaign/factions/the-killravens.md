# The Killravens (Killraven Crime League)

**Type:** Rising criminal organization
**Seat:** Killraven's Tower, North Market (appeared 720 IA — one year ago).

## Goals
- Carve a criminal empire out of Balacazar territory, fast.
- Control the flow of a specific commodity (drugs, chaositech, information — GM's call as the arc develops).
- Establish Kevris Killraven as a power the city's other factions must reckon with.

## Current activities
- Aggressive expansion into the Docks and Warrens — muscle, bribes, and the occasional disappearance.
- Recruiting ex-sailors, gangs, and desperate talent the Balacazars overlooked.
- Probing for weaknesses in Balacazar fronts and Watch coverage.

## Leadership
- **Kevris Killraven** — enigmatic crime lord; arrived in Ptolus in 720 IA. Rumored to be more than human. Operates from her tower; few have met her and talked about it (Ptolus p. 315).

## Disposition toward the party
*Starting: neutral/opportunistic.* Killraven hires deniable talent and rewards results. She has no loyalty to spare and a long memory for betrayal.

## Relationships
- **Rivals:** the [[balacazars]] (the central gang war of the early campaign).
- **Wildcard:** her true nature and backers are unknown — a thread worth pulling.

## Notes
The Killraven–Balacazar war is a renewable engine of jobs, chaos, and moral choices for the party. *(Ptolus p. 315.)*
