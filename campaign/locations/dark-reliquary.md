# The Dark Reliquary

**District:** The Necropolis.
**Controlled by:** the [[../factions/the-forsaken]] and the Fallen.

## Read-aloud
*An enormous, brooding manor house rises among the tombs, its windows dark but never quite empty. The air around it is colder than the night should allow, and the silence has a held-breath quality — as if the house is listening back.*

## Key features
- A grand manor above ground with **at least three dungeon levels** below.
- Home to the **Fallen** — actual demons, including the demon lords **Raguel and Lilith** — who direct the mortal Forsaken.
- The **Wintersouled** undead serve as guards and shock troops.
- Heavily warded and watched; entering uninvited is a death sentence.

## Inhabitants
- The Fallen (demons), the Forsaken (mortal cultists), Wintersouled undead. See [[../factions/the-forsaken]].

## Role in the campaign
The central antagonist location of the **Night of Dissolution**. Early on it is a place of rumor and dread; by the late arcs it is the source of the city's doom. Tie it to the City Turn "Awaken the Galchutt" / "Open the Banewarrens" clocks.

## Notes
The City Watch does not patrol inside the Necropolis — the party is on its own here, especially after dark. *(Ptolus p. 232.)*
