# Castle Shard

**District:** Nobles' Quarter.
**Held by:** [[../npcs/lord-zavere]] and Lady Rill.

## Read-aloud
*A keep unlike any other in the Quarter — grown, not merely built, around a vast shard of crystallized magic that pulses faintly at its heart. Inside, the halls run farther than the outer walls should allow, and the very stone seems aware of you.*

## Key features
- The keep is itself a **sentient magical artifact**, bound to a great shard of crystallized magic; its interior is larger than its exterior.
- Once a seat of ancient evil, now a force for good under Lord Zavere.
- Access is by **invitation only**; Zavere can vouch for worthy adventurers.

## Inhabitants
- Lord Zavere, Lady Rill, and a capable household of agents and guards.

## Role in the campaign
The party's most powerful potential **ally** against House Vladaam and the chaos cults. Earning Zavere's trust — or a standing as agents (even lords) of Castle Shard — is a campaign-level achievement and a recurring source of aid, information, and missions.

## Notes
Zavere opposes the Vladaams and the Forsaken openly enough to be a target. *(Ptolus p. 285.)*
