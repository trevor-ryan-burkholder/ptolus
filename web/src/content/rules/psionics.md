# Psionics — GM Reference

*Mechanics extracted from Expanded Psionics Handbook (EPH). Default rule: psionics–magic transparency is ON. All page refs are EPH unless noted.*

---

## The Basics

A **power** is a one-time psionic effect, analogous to a spell. A manifester knows powers and has a daily reserve of **power points (PP)** to pay for them. Unlike prepared spells, any known power can be manifested any number of times per day as long as PP remain.

**Key ability scores:**
| Class | Key ability | PP source |
|---|---|---|
| Psion | Intelligence | Int bonus powers (Table 2–1) |
| Psychic warrior | Wisdom | Wis bonus powers |
| Wilder | Charisma | Cha bonus powers |

**Minimum ability to manifest:** 10 + power level. A psion needs Int 15 to manifest 5th-level powers.

### Wilder
Same PP as psion but drawn from Cha. All known powers come from the psion/wilder list. Wild Surge adds +1 to +5 manifester level but risks psychic enervation (Will DC 15 + surge bonus or lose PP equal to power cost, and cannot manifest next round).

## Manifesting a Power

**Action:** 1 standard action (most powers). Some are 1 round, 1 minute, swift action, or immediate action.

**Provoking AoO:** Manifesting from a power list provokes AoO like casting a spell (move to the threatened creature counts, not the manifestation itself). Manifest defensively: Concentration DC 15 + power level, success = no AoO.

**Concentration checks** (DC = base + power level):
- Damaged during manifestation: DC 10 + damage dealt + PL
- Grappled/pinned: DC 20 + PL
- Vigorous motion: DC 10 + PL
- Manifest without display: DC 15 + PL
- Entangled: DC 15

**Save DC:** 10 + power level + key ability modifier (Int for psion, Wis for psychic warrior, Cha for wilder).

**Manifester level (ML):** = psionic class level. Determines range, duration, augmentation cap. ML check to beat power resistance: d20 + ML vs. creature's PR.

**Regaining PP:** 8 hours of rest + 1 round of concentration. All PP return at once (not per hour). Interruptions add 1 hour each.

## Psionic Focus

Psionic focus is a special state maintained by holding PP in mind. It powers many psionic feats and has other tactical uses.

**Gaining focus:** Concentration check DC 20, full-round action that provokes AoO. Must have ≥1 PP available. Once focused, you stay focused until you expend focus, go to 0 PP, fall unconscious, or sleep.

**Expending focus:** When you expend focus on a Concentration check, treat the d20 as if you rolled a 15 (add that to your modifier instead of rolling). Many psionic feats require expending focus to activate (e.g., Psionic Shot, Psionic Charge, Psionic Fist, Twin Power, Widen Power).

**Psicrystal bonus:** If you have a psicrystal, its personality grants a skill bonus (e.g., +3 on Concentration). Psicrystals count as part of you for purposes of personal-range powers.

---

## Augmentation

Many powers can be **augmented** by spending additional PP when manifesting. The Augment entry in a power's description specifies cost and effect.

**Cap:** You cannot spend more total PP on a power than your manifester level. A 7th-level psion (ML 7) manifesting energy bolt (3pp base) can augment it with up to 4 more PP for 7 total.

Augmentation is decided at the moment of manifestation; you cannot augment after the fact. Powers without an Augment entry cannot be augmented.

## Psionics–Magic Transparency (Default)

Unless the DM uses the Variant: Psionics Is Different rule (EPH p. 65), the following apply:

- **Spell resistance = power resistance.** SR protects against powers; PR protects against spells. A creature with one has the other at the same value.
- **Dispel magic vs. dispel psionics** work identically across each other's effects (same mechanics, same check).
- **detect magic detects powers** (Psicraft check to identify discipline of the aura).
- **Dead magic areas = dead psionic areas.**
- **Stacking:** Armor bonus from *inertial armor* and *mage armor* do NOT stack (same bonus type). Armor and insight bonuses from different sources DO stack.
- **School equivalents:**
  - Clairsentience = Divination
  - Metacreativity = Conjuration
  - Psychokinesis = Evocation
  - Psychometabolism = Transmutation
  - Telepathy = Enchantment
  - Psychoportation = no equivalent school

**Variant (Psionics Is Different):** Dispel magic cannot affect psionic powers and vice versa. SR doesn't protect against powers; PR doesn't protect against spells. Elf SR bonus doesn't apply to telepathy powers. Dead magic zones don't suppress psionics. Use only if you want psionics as "alien energy."

## Power Resistance

PR works exactly like SR (d20 + ML ≥ PR to overcome). By default (transparency ON), SR and PR are identical — a creature with SR 18 also has PR 18.

Powers noted as "(harmless)" or targeting only "You" do not trigger PR. A creature can voluntarily lower its own PR (standard action).

## Disciplines

Six disciplines group related powers. A psion specializes in one, gaining access to discipline-only powers and bonus skills:

| Discipline | Specialist | School Equivalent | Focus |
|---|---|---|---|
| Clairsentience | Seer | Divination | Perception, prediction, information |
| Metacreativity | Shaper | Conjuration | Creating matter, constructs, ectoplasm |
| Psychokinesis | Kineticist | Evocation | Energy, matter manipulation, damage |
| Psychometabolism | Egoist | Transmutation | Healing, physical transformation |
| Psychoportation | Nomad | (none) | Movement, teleportation, time |
| Telepathy | Telepath | Enchantment | Mind reading, control, communication |

A psion **cannot** use powers from disciplines other than their own, even from items (exception: feats like Expanded Knowledge).

---

## Psi-Like Abilities

Psionic creatures often have psi-like abilities (Ps): function like powers but cost no PP, require no material components, and are not subject to disruption. Manifesting time 1 standard action unless noted. Subject to power resistance and dispel psionics. Don't function in null psionics fields.

Stat block notation: "3/day—mind thrust (5d10, DC 13\*)" — the asterisk means the power is pre-augmented to the creature's ML; the save DC is already computed.

## Key Differences from Spellcasting

| Factor | Spellcasting | Psionics |
|---|---|---|
| Preparation | Wizards prepare slots; sorcerers known list | All psions use known-list like sorcerers |
| Resource unit | Spell slots by level | Power points (unified pool) |
| Scaling | Casting higher-level spell | Augmenting with more PP |
| Components | V, S, M, F, DF | None by default; displays are cosmetic (suppressible) |
| Save DC key ability | Int (wiz), Cha (sor/brd), Wis (div/clr) | Int (psion), Wis (psy. war.), Cha (wilder) |
| Action economy | Standard (most), 1 round, free | Same + swift/immediate action powers exist |
| Cross-resistance | SR vs. spells | PR vs. powers; = SR under transparency |
| Multiclass pooling | Separate spell slots | PP pools combine across psionic classes |
| Dispelling | CL check vs. dispel DC | ML check vs. dispel DC; same math |
| "Cantrips" | 0-level, free | 1pp powers (cheap but never free) |
| Combat noise | Visible V/S components | Displays (optional, can suppress; DC 15+PL) |

## Metapsionic Feats

Metapsionic feats modify powers, most requiring **expending psionic focus** and increasing PP cost. Key examples:

| Feat | PP Cost | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Empower Power | +2 | All variable numeric effects ×1.5 |
| Maximize Power | +4 | All variable numeric effects maximized |
| Twin Power | +6 | Power takes effect twice on same target/area |
| Extend Power | +2 | Double duration |
| Widen Power | +4 | Double area dimensions (burst/spread/cone/line) |
| Split Psionic Ray | +2 | Ray hits two targets within 30 ft. of each other |
| Quicken Power | +6 | Manifest as swift action (no AoO) |
| Unconditional Power | +8 | Manifest while dazed/confused/stunned/etc. |

All metapsionic feats: total PP cost cannot exceed manifester level.

## Power Failure

A power fails and PP are wasted if:
- Concentration is broken
- Target type is wrong (e.g., charm used on a non-humanoid)
- Power cannot conform to range/area constraints
- You lack the minimum ability score (10 + PL)

Suppressed (not wasted) in null psionics field or antimagic field (under transparency).

## Psicrystals

Created via Psicrystal Affinity feat. Fragment of the manifester's personality in construct form (Diminutive, hardness 8, half master's HP, AC 16). Communicates telepathically with master (1 mile range).

Grants: +3 on a skill (based on personality chosen), alertness when within arm's reach, improved evasion, share powers (master can manifest personal powers on psicrystal), sighted (60 ft. blindsight via telepathic link), self-propulsion (Str 1, Dex 15, Speed 30/climb 20 when activated). CR included with master.

Key use: Deliver touch powers. Expend from a safe distance by directing psicrystal to make the touch.

## Psionic Cascade (Disease: Cascade Flu)

- **Source:** Brain mole bite, other psionic vermin
- **Type:** Injury; DC 13 Fort; 1-day incubation
- **Effect:** Each time character manifests a power, make DC 16 Concentration or trigger cascade. On cascade: power manifests normally, then random known powers manifest uncontrolled each subsequent round (2 next round, 3 the round after, etc.) draining all PP. Touch/personal powers hit manifester; others target randomly (d% determines direction).
