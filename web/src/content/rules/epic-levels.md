# Epic Levels (21+)
**Source:** Epic Level Handbook (ELH)

---

## Core Mechanic Changes at 21+

Epic characters (character level 21+) continue advancing but under modified rules.

**Base Attack Bonus / Base Save Bonus:** Both stop increasing after character level 20. Instead:
- **Epic Attack Bonus:** +1 at 21st, then +1 every odd level (21, 23, 25, 27, 29...) — same for all classes.
- **Epic Save Bonus:** +1 at 22nd, then +1 every even level (22, 24, 26, 28, 30...) — same for all classes. Applies to all three saves equally.
- These epic bonuses stack with all normal bonuses when determining attack/save totals, but do NOT grant additional attacks per round.

| Level | Epic Save Bonus | Epic Attack Bonus |
|-------|----------------|-------------------|
| 21    | +0             | +1                |
| 22    | +1             | +1                |
| 23    | +1             | +2                |
| 24    | +2             | +2                |
| 25    | +2             | +3                |
| 26    | +3             | +3                |
| 27    | +3             | +4                |
| 28    | +4             | +4                |
| 29    | +4             | +5                |
| 30    | +5             | +5                |

**Hit Dice and Skill Points:** Continue to accumulate at normal class rate.

**Class Features:** Any feature that scales with class level (lay on hands, stunning fist DC, bardic knowledge, sneak attack dice, etc.) keeps scaling. Exception: features with a hard cap (monk unarmed damage stops at 16th, rogue special abilities stop at 19th) do not improve.

**Spells Per Day:** Do NOT increase after 20th level. Caster level keeps increasing. Only the Improved Spell Capacity feat adds higher spell slots.

**Familiars/Special Mounts/Fiendish Servants:** Continue to improve per their progression tables.

---

## Universal Epic Progression

**Feats:** One feat (epic or nonepic, player's choice) at every level divisible by 3 (21, 24, 27, 30...). These are in addition to class bonus feats.

**Ability Score Increases:** +1 to one ability score at every level divisible by 4 (20, 24, 28...). The 20th level increase counts; the next is at 24th.

**Skill Max Ranks:** Equal to character level + 3 for class skills; half that for cross-class.

---

## Epic Bonus Feats (Class-Specific)

Each class gains bonus epic feats from a class-specific list at a set interval (separate from the universal feat-per-3-levels):
- Fighter: every 2 levels after 20 (22, 24, 26...)
- Wizard, Cleric, Bard, Sorcerer, Druid, Ranger, Paladin: every 3 levels after 20
- Rogue: every 4 levels after 20
- Barbarian: every 4 levels after 20
- Monk: every 5 levels after 20

All epic bonus feats must be selected from that class's bonus feat list (or the universal epic feat list where allowed). Characters also get the nonepic/epic feat every 3rd character level independently.

## Epic Spellcasting

Requires the **Epic Spellcasting** feat. Epic spells are separate from normal spells and use their own slot pool.

**Epic Spell Slots per Day:** Number of ranks in Knowledge (arcana) for arcane casters, or Knowledge (religion)/(nature) for divine casters, divided by 10 (round down). A caster with 24 ranks in Knowledge (arcana) gets 2 arcane epic spell slots/day.

**Casting:** Make a **Spellcraft check vs. the spell's Spellcraft DC**. Success = spell fires; failure = slot used, spell fizzles. Casters typically take 10. A spell is uncastable if its final DC exceeds 20 + the caster's Spellcraft modifier.

**Epic Spell Level:** All epic spells count as 10th-level for Concentration checks, spell resistance, and similar purposes.

**Saving Throw DC:** 20 + relevant ability modifier (can be increased via factors during development).

**Development:** Must spend gold, time, and XP to develop each epic spell before it can be cast. Cost varies by DC. Once developed, it is known permanently.

**Default Casting Time:** 1 minute (can be reduced to 1 action via the +20 DC factor — a common cost in practice).

**Metamagic:** Cannot be applied to epic spells. Epic spells cannot be placed in magic items.

## Epic Spell Seeds

Epic spells are built from **seeds** (base effects) modified by **factors** (DC adjustments). Key seeds:

| Seed     | Base DC | Effect Summary |
|----------|---------|----------------|
| Afflict  | 14      | Penalties on attacks/saves/ability scores |
| Animate  | 25      | Animate object/matter |
| Animate Dead | 23 | Create/control undead |
| Armor    | 14      | AC bonus |
| Banish   | 27      | Banish extraplanar creatures |
| Compel   | 19      | Compulsion effect |
| Conjure  | varies  | Summon creatures |
| Destroy  | 29      | Deal 20d6 damage to target |
| Energy   | 19      | Energy blast (10d6 of one type in bolt) |
| Fortify  | 17      | Bonus to AC, saves, or ability scores |
| Heal     | 25      | Heal up to 210 hp |
| Life     | 27      | Restore life (resurrection) |
| Reflect  | 27      | Redirect spells/effects back |
| Slay     | 25      | Kill one creature outright |
| Summon   | 14      | Summon creatures |
| Transform | 21    | Polymorph/alter form |
| Transport | 27    | Teleportation/planar travel |
| Ward     | 14      | Protective aura/warding |

**Mitigating Factors** reduce DC: longer casting time (−20 DC for 10 minutes, −200 DC for 100 days), ritual casters each contributing spell slots, XP burn (−1 DC per 100 XP burned), backlash damage to caster (−1 DC per d6).

## Sample Epic Spells

### Animus Blizzard
- **Spellcraft DC:** 78
- **Development:** 702,000 gp; 15 days; 28,080 XP
- **Casting Time:** 1 minute | **Range:** 300 ft. | **Area:** 20-ft.-radius hemisphere
- **Effect:** 30d6 cold damage (Reflex half). Up to 5 victims who die animate as wights under your permanent control.

### Dragon Knight (Ritual)
- **Spellcraft DC:** 38
- **Development:** 342,000 gp; 7 days; 13,680 XP
- **Casting Time:** 1 action (ritual requires additional casters) | **Range:** 75 ft.
- **Effect:** Summons one adult red dragon that serves you for 20 rounds. Ritual spell — requires additional casters contributing spell slots.

### Hellball
- **Spellcraft DC:** 90
- **Development:** 810,000 gp; 17 days; 32,400 XP
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 300 ft. | **Area:** 40-ft.-radius spread | **XP Cost:** 400 XP per casting
- **Effect:** 10d6 acid + 10d6 fire + 10d6 electricity + 10d6 sonic damage (Reflex half). Caster takes 10d6 backlash damage.

### Kinetic Control
- **Spellcraft DC:** 103
- **Development:** 927,000 gp; 19 days; 37,080 XP
- **Casting Time:** 1 minute | **Duration:** 12 hours or until discharged
- **Effect:** Absorb up to 20 points per attack from slashing, bludgeoning, and piercing damage (up to 150 points total stored). As a free action, discharge stored damage onto any one creature you touch, dealing it all at once.

### Momento Mori
- **Spellcraft DC:** 86 (note: not 85 as listed in DC table — description gives 86)
- **Development:** 774,000 gp; 16 days; 30,960 XP
- **Casting Time:** 1 quickened action | **Components:** None | **Range:** 300 ft.
- **Effect:** A thought that kills. One living creature must make a Fortitude save or die instantly. No verbal or somatic components — no way to identify the caster. Caster takes 10d6 backlash.
