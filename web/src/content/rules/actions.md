# Actions in Combat

Each round, a character gets one **standard action**, one **move action**, plus free actions and (usually) one **swift action**. Or one **full-round action** in place of standard + move.

## Standard actions

- **Attack** (melee or ranged)
- **Cast spell** (1-action casting time)
- **Activate magic item** (most wondrous items)
- **Use special ability** (most class powers; varies)
- **Total defense** (+4 dodge AC, no attacks)
- **Aid another** (+2 to ally's attack or AC)
- **Bull rush**, **disarm**, **sunder**, **trip** (each can be a standard or as part of melee attack)
- **Ready an action** (specify trigger; act when it occurs)
- **Delay** (act later in initiative)

## Move actions

- **Move up to speed**
- **Draw or sheathe a weapon** (free with BAB +1 if part of move)
- **Stand from prone** (provokes AoO)
- **Retrieve stored item** (provokes AoO)
- **Open door**, **load light crossbow**, **mount horse**
- **Manipulate item** (most fine actions)

## Full-round actions

- **Full attack** (multiple iterative attacks; no movement except 5-ft. step)
- **Charge** (2× speed move + attack at +2/-2 AC)
- **Run** (4× speed, ×3 in heavy armor)
- **Cast a 1-round spell** (most longer-casting spells)
- **Coup de grace** (auto-crit on helpless foe; massive damage save vs. instant death)

## Free actions

- **Speak** (a few sentences)
- **Drop an item or prone**
- **Cease concentration on a spell**
- **5-foot step** (once per round; you cannot also take other movement)

## Swift and immediate actions

- **Swift action**: 1 per round, used for fast spell metamagic, some class abilities.
- **Immediate action**: like a swift but can be used on someone else's turn; counts as your next swift.

## Common conversions

- Drawing a sheathed weapon: move action (free with BAB +1 if combined with move).
- Drinking a potion: standard action; provokes AoO.
- Reloading: light crossbow = move; heavy = full-round; sling = move; longbow = free.
- Picking up an item: move action (provokes if from another square).
