# Mounted Combat (3.5e)

## Basics

- **Mount must be one size category larger** than the rider (a Medium rider needs a Large mount).
- **Controlling the mount in combat** requires a Ride check (see DCs below).
- **Without a heavy warhorse training:** light or riding horse will fight defensively only; it may bolt, shy, or freeze.
- A **trained war mount** (war-trained horse or warhorse) can enter combat without a Ride check to stay in the fight.

## Ride Skill DCs (Quick Reference)

| Action | DC | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Guide with knees | 5 | Lets you use both hands freely; must do this to use a shield or two-handed weapon |
| Stay in saddle | 5 | Automatic for trained riders in normal conditions |
| Fight with combat-trained mount | 10 | Per round when in melee; handle animal (unntrained = DC 20) |
| Cover (duck to one side) | 15 | Gain +4 AC and ½ cover; can only Spot DC 10 |
| Soft fall | 15 | Take no damage when falling from mount (fail = 1d6 fall) |
| Leap | 15 | Mount jumps obstacle; rider doesn't fall off |
| Spur mount past fear | 15 | Move mount despite a fear source in its path |
| Fast mount/dismount | 20 | Mount or dismount as free action; fail = normal action |
| Controlling an untrained mount | 20 | Animals not trained for combat |

All Ride checks are a **free action** unless otherwise noted. Fail by 5+ = fall (1d6 per 5 ft.; usually 5 ft. = 1d6 damage).

## Mounted Charge

1. The mount uses the **Double Move action** (minimum 10 ft.) in a straight line ending at the target.
2. Rider makes a single melee attack at **+2 to attack rolls** (the charge bonus).
3. **Lances deal double damage on a mounted charge** (lance is the quintessential mounted weapon).
4. After the charge, both rider and mount are in the square the attack was made from.
5. The mount **provokes AoOs** while moving through threatened squares unless the mount has 5-foot-step options or the ride-by attack feat.

**Ride-By Attack (feat):** Rider can attack and continue moving after the attack. Does not provoke an AoO from the attacked target.

**Spirited Charge (feat):** With lance, deal ×3 damage on a mounted charge (or ×4 if combined with Ride-By Attack vs a target that is also charging).

## Mounted Archery

- Firing a ranged weapon while mounted: **-4 penalty** on attack rolls (reduces to -2 if Mounted Archery feat).
- Must use Guide with Knees (DC 5) to use both hands for a bow; or use Ride to control with knees.
- Cannot use a two-handed weapon while using the mount's movement in the same round (unless Ride check DC 5 passed).

## Mount Movement and Rider

- When the mount moves, the rider moves with it — the rider is not using their own movement.
- The rider can still take their **standard action** (attack, spell, etc.) while the mount uses its movement.
- If the mount takes a **double move** (two move actions), the rider can still take a **swift or immediate action** but loses their standard action.
- If the mount takes a **full attack**, the rider can also take a **full attack** at -2 penalty (they share the mount's action economy somewhat — in practice this means a rider can full-attack at the cost of the mount's movement).

## Mounted Combat Feat

**Mounted Combat (feat):** Once per round, when mount is hit with an attack, rider may make a Ride check (DC = attacker's attack roll result) to negate the hit. This is a reaction.

**Warhorse vs. riding horse:** Any horse can carry a rider. A warhorse (trained for war) won't flee from combat creatures — it fights on command. A riding horse may bolt (Will DC 15 or Reflex DC 15 situation-dependent) when in melee with aggressive creatures. Use Handle Animal to calm a bolting mount (DC 20).

## Falling from the Saddle

- Involuntary (knocked unconscious, bull rushed, etc.): Ride DC 20 to stay in saddle.
- Mount killed: Fall 5 ft. for 1d6 damage unless Ride DC 15 (soft fall).
- Unconscious rider falls automatically unless secured in saddle.

## Lance Rules

- **Lance:** 1d8 damage, ×3 crit, reach weapon (10 ft.). You can attack an adjacent foe with a -4 penalty.
- Double damage on mounted charge (+×2 multiplier; with lance's ×3, combined = ×4 if both apply).
- Reach: Can attack 10 ft. away (not adjacent).

*Sources: PHB 75-76 (Ride skill), PHB 113 (Lance), DMG 20, MM 273-274 (horse stats).*
