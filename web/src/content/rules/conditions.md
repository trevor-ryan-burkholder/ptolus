# Conditions

Quick reference. When a condition applies, look it up here at the table.

**Ability Damaged**: Loses 1 point per damage. If a save's ability is reduced to 0, the character is helpless/unconscious depending on which.

**Ability Drained**: Like damage but permanent until restored with magic.

**Blinded**: Lose Dex bonus to AC, -2 AC, 50% miss chance on attacks, can't make AoOs, takes -4 on Str/Dex skills and most Search checks, half speed.

**Confused**: Roll d% each round — 01-10 attack self; 11-20 babble; 21-50 do nothing; 51-70 flee from caster; 71-100 attack nearest creature.

**Cowering**: Frozen in fear, drops anything held, loses Dex bonus, -2 AC, can take no actions. Foes get +2 attack.

**Dazed**: Lose all actions for the round (no attacks, spells, etc.). Can still defend (keeps Dex bonus).

**Dazzled**: -1 attack rolls, -1 Search and Spot.

**Deafened**: -4 initiative, 20% spell failure on spells with V components, can't hear, can't make sound-based Listen checks.

**Disabled**: At 0 HP. Takes one standard or move action per turn, no full attacks. Activity causes 1 HP damage.

**Dying**: -1 to -9 HP, unconscious. Each round, 10% chance to stabilize on own.

**Energy Drained**: Each negative level: -1 attack, -1 saves, -1 effective level, -5 HP, -1 skill/ability checks. 24h after, Fortitude save (DC 10 + ½ source HD + source Cha mod) or it becomes permanent ability loss.

**Entangled**: Half speed, can't run/charge, -2 attack, -4 Dex, concentration check DC 15 to cast.

**Exhausted**: ½ speed, -6 Str and Dex. Becomes fatigued after 1 hour rest.

**Fatigued**: Can't run or charge, -2 Str and Dex. Recovers after 8 hours rest.

**Flat-Footed**: Lose Dex bonus to AC and can't make AoOs (until you've acted in combat).

**Frightened**: Flees from source if possible. -2 attack, save, skill, ability while fleeing. (Stronger than shaken, weaker than panicked.)

**Grappled**: Lose Dex bonus vs. non-grapplers, can't move freely, can only act with grapple checks.

**Helpless**: Bound, sleeping, paralyzed, unconscious. Treated as Dex 0 (-5 AC), foes can coup de grace.

**Incorporeal**: No physical body. Immune to non-magical attacks. 50% miss chance vs. magic weapons (except force or by other incorporeal).

**Invisible**: Attackers have 50% miss chance, +2 attack, denies Dex to AC. Tracked via Move Silently/Listen, Spot. See *Invisibility* spell.

**Nauseated**: Single move action per round, no attacks/spells/concentration.

**Panicked**: Flees at top speed, drops items, -2 attack/save/etc. Can't take actions other than fleeing.

**Paralyzed**: Frozen, helpless. Can take purely mental actions only.

**Petrified**: Turned to stone. Effectively dead; can be restored with stone to flesh.

**Pinned**: A specific grapple state. Helpless to non-grapplers, can only attempt to escape.

**Prone**: -4 attack with melee, +4 AC vs. ranged, -4 AC vs. melee. Standing provokes AoO.

**Shaken**: -2 attack, save, skill, ability checks. (Weakest fear condition.)

**Sickened**: -2 attack, weapon damage, save, skill, ability checks.

**Stable**: At -1 to -9 HP but no longer losing HP. Still unconscious.

**Staggered**: Standard OR move action per round (not both), no full attack.

**Stunned**: Drops anything held, can't take actions, -2 AC, loses Dex bonus.

**Unconscious**: Helpless.

## Fear progression

Shaken → Frightened → Panicked. A creature already shaken by a new shaken effect becomes frightened, etc.
