## Load Effects

| Load | Speed (30) | Speed (20) | Max Dex | ACP | Run |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Light | 30 ft. | 20 ft. | — | — | ×4 |
| Medium | 20 ft. | 15 ft. | +3 | -3 | ×4 |
| Heavy | 20 ft. | 15 ft. | +1 | -6 | ×3 |

Medium and heavy loads also impose **check penalty on Str- and Dex-based skills**.

## Typical Adventurer Loadouts

**Light fighter (leather/chain shirt):** Armor 15-25, shield 5-15, weapons 4-8, pack 20-30 = ~50-80 lb. Fine for Str 12+.

**Heavy fighter (plate/chainmail):** Armor 40-50, shield 15-45, weapons 8-12, pack 15-25 = ~80-130 lb. Needs Str 16+ for Light load; most settle for Medium.

**Rogue (no armor or leather):** Armor 0-15, weapons 5-8, pack 20-30 = ~25-50 lb. Str 10 can run Light easily.

**Wizard:** No armor, a few light weapons, spellbook 3 lb, pack 20-30 = ~30-40 lb.
