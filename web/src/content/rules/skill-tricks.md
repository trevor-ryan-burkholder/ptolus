# Skill Tricks (Complete Scoundrel)

Source: *Complete Scoundrel* (CS), Chapter 2. All skill tricks cost **2 skill points** each. Each trick can be used once per encounter unless noted. Requirements are minimum ranks in the listed skill(s); the trick becomes available the moment you meet the requirement.

Skill tricks are a **swift action** to activate unless the description specifies otherwise. They use the listed skill to determine success where applicable.

---

## Acrobatic Tricks

| Trick | Skill Req | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Acrobatic Backstab** | Tumble 12 | After using Tumble to move through an enemy's space, you may treat that enemy as flanked for your next attack this round. |
| **Back on Your Feet** | Tumble 12 | When knocked prone, stand up as a free action (instead of a move action) once per encounter. |
| **Extreme Leap** | Jump 5, Tumble 5 | After a successful Jump check (horizontal jump), move an additional 10 ft. as part of that move. |
| **Nimble Charge** | Balance 5 | Charge across difficult terrain without penalty to movement or the charge itself. |
| **Twisted Charge** | Balance 5, Tumble 5 | When charging, you may make one turn of up to 90 degrees during the charge. |

---

## Balance & Movement Tricks

| Trick | Skill Req | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Leaping Climber** | Climb 5, Jump 5 | After a successful Jump check made as part of a move action, you may make a Climb check to catch a wall, ledge, or similar surface as a free action without a running start. |
| **Ledge Walker** | Balance 5 | Move along narrow surfaces at full speed (instead of half) without penalty; no Balance check needed for surfaces 4 in. or wider. |
| **Speedy Ascent** | Climb 12 | Climb at full speed (instead of half); make two Climb checks per round of climbing. |
| **Walk the Walls** | Balance 12, Climb 12 | For one round, run along a vertical surface as if it were the ground (speed unchanged; must end turn on normal ground or fall). |

## Combat Tricks

| Trick | Skill Req | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Clarity of Vision** | Spot 12 | As a swift action, pinpoint one invisible creature within 30 ft. you know is present; ignore concealment for attacks against that target until end of turn. |
| **Clever Improvisation** | Use Magic Device 5 | Use any improvised weapon as a martial weapon (proficiency, no nonproficiency penalty) for one round. |
| **Distracting Feint** | Bluff 6, Sleight of Hand 6 | Attempt a Bluff feint as a swift action (instead of a standard action); the feint denies Dex bonus to the next attack only. |
| **Feign Weakness** | Bluff 8 | Deliberately miss an attack to grant the enemy a false sense of security; that enemy is flat-footed against your next attack before the end of your next turn. |
| **Hit and Run** | Tumble 8 | After making a melee attack, you may immediately move 5 ft. without provoking an AoO. This is in addition to any 5-ft. step. |
| **Opportunistic Strike** | Sense Motive 5 | When any creature is denied its Dex bonus to AC for any reason, you may make an AoO against it even if the trigger did not normally allow one (limit once/round). |
| **Throw Splash Weapon** | Sleight of Hand 5 | When throwing a splash weapon, you may choose to have the splash affect only your designated target (no splash to adjacent squares). |

## Escape & Grapple Tricks

| Trick | Skill Req | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Escape Artist** | Escape Artist 8 | As a swift action, attempt to slip free from a grapple or pin (opposed Escape Artist vs. grappler's grapple check). |
| **Slipping Past** | Escape Artist 12 | Move through the space of a creature at least one size larger than you without provoking AoOs. |

## Knowledge & Perception Tricks

| Trick | Skill Req | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Collector of Stories** | Knowledge (any) 5 | When making a Knowledge check to identify a monster or recall lore about a specific person or place, you may reroll the check once and take the better result. |
| **Never Outnumbered** | Intimidate 8 | Use Intimidate to demoralize all enemies within 10 ft. simultaneously (standard action; normal rules per target still apply for duration and success). |
| **Point It Out** | Spot 10 | As a swift action, indicate a hidden creature you have spotted — one ally of your choice can immediately make a free Spot check to notice the same creature. |

## Social Tricks

| Trick | Skill Req | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Assume Quirk** | Disguise 5 | Adopt a minor physical quirk (limp, accent, nervous habit) to make a disguise harder to see through; grant +4 bonus on Disguise check vs. anyone who has seen through your disguises before. |
| **Conceal Spellcasting** | Bluff 5, Concentration 5 | Opponents cannot attempt a Spellcraft check to identify a spell you are casting unless they succeed on a Spot check (DC 10+Bluff modifier). |
| **False Theurgy** | Spellcraft 9, Bluff 5 | When casting a spell, disguise it as a different spell of your choice; observers must succeed on a Spellcraft check (DC 15 + your Bluff modifier) to identify the real spell. |
| **Group Fake-Out** | Bluff 8, Sense Motive 8 | Make one Bluff check to feint two adjacent enemies simultaneously; requires one attack against each target. |
| **Persuasive Performance** | Perform (any) 5 | Make a Perform check in place of a Diplomacy check for one specific request or attitude adjustment; takes the same time as Diplomacy. |
| **Social Recovery** | Bluff 8, Diplomacy 8 | After a Diplomacy check failure that shifts attitude negatively, immediately retry once at DC+5; can only use once per encounter with a given creature. |
| **Spur On** | Diplomacy 6 | As a swift action, grant one ally within 30 ft. a +2 morale bonus on their next skill check this round. |

## Stealth Tricks

| Trick | Skill Req | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Corner Perch** | Balance 5, Climb 5 | As a swift action, perch in a corner or overhead position; gain +4 bonus on Hide checks and adjacent squares count as difficult terrain for enemies attempting to reach you. |
| **Disappearing Act** | Sleight of Hand 8 | Once per encounter, when you have cover or concealment, you may attempt a Hide check as an immediate action when an enemy gains line of sight to you. |
| **Hidden Blade** | Sleight of Hand 5, Sense Motive 5 | Draw a light weapon as a free action with a Sleight of Hand check (DC 20) — observers do not notice the drawn weapon unless they succeed on a Spot check opposed by your Sleight of Hand. |
| **Listening Loophole** | Listen 5 | Listen to a conversation through a door or wall as a swift action; reduce the Listen check DC by 5. |
| **Stealthy Movement** | Move Silently 5 | Move at full speed with no penalty to Move Silently checks (normally −5 for full speed movement). |

---

## Mounted & Special Tricks

| Trick | Skill Req | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Mounted Assault** | Ride 10 | When making a mounted charge, your mount may make one additional move action (not attack) as part of the charge without provoking AoOs. |
| **Sudden Draw** | Sleight of Hand 5 | Draw a weapon as a free action (instead of a move action) once per round; can be done as part of a move action. |
