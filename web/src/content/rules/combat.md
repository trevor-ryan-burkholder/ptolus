# Combat (3.5e Reference)

## Sequence

1. **Surprise round** (only if some combatants are unaware). Aware combatants get one standard action only.
2. **Initiative**: each combatant rolls d20 + Dex mod (+ feats, etc.). Highest goes first.
3. **Combat rounds**: each combatant acts on their initiative count. A round = 6 seconds and includes everyone's turn.
4. Combat ends when one side is defeated, surrenders, or flees.

## Attack rolls

`d20 + BAB + ability mod + size mod + misc`

- **Melee**: Str mod
- **Ranged**: Dex mod
- **Finesse weapons** (with Weapon Finesse feat): Dex instead of Str
- Hit if total ≥ target's AC. Natural 20 always hits; natural 1 always misses.

## Critical hits

On natural roll within crit range (19–20 / 18–20 / etc.), confirm: roll another attack. If that also hits AC, multiply damage dice (×2, ×3, ×4 depending on weapon).

- Damage that isn't dice (Str mod, enhancement) is multiplied too.
- Bonus dice (sneak attack, flaming) are **not** multiplied — added once.

## Armor Class

`10 + armor + shield + Dex (capped by armor max) + size + natural + deflection + dodge + misc`

Tracked three ways:
- **Total AC**: normal
- **Touch AC**: ignores armor, shield, natural armor; keeps Dex, dodge, deflection, size
- **Flat-footed AC**: ignores Dex and dodge bonuses (you've not yet acted in combat, or are caught off guard)

## Damage and HP

- Damage subtracts from HP. At 0 HP: disabled (can take 1 standard or move action, then drop to -1).
- –1 to –9 HP: dying (lose 1 HP per round until stabilized or healed).
- –10 HP: dead.
- Massive damage (50+ damage from a single hit): Fortitude DC 15 or die regardless of HP.

## Saving throws

`d20 + base save + ability mod + misc`

- **Fortitude** (Con): poison, disease, paralysis, energy drain
- **Reflex** (Dex): area effects, fire breath, lightning, falling
- **Will** (Wis): mind effects, charm, fear, illusions

Natural 1 always fails. Natural 20 always succeeds.

## Iterative attacks (full attack)

A character with BAB +6 or higher gets a second attack at -5; BAB +11 third at -10; BAB +16 fourth at -15. Must take a full-attack action.

## Concealment

- 20% miss chance: concealment (shadowy illumination, light fog).
- 50% miss chance: total concealment (darkness vs. no darkvision, dense fog, invisibility). Attacker also can't pick the square — must guess.

## Cover

- +4 AC, +2 Reflex: partial cover (e.g., low wall).
- Improved cover: +8 AC, +4 Reflex.
- Total cover: can't be targeted directly.

## Flanking

Two allies on opposite sides of a target: each gets +2 attack. Required for rogues to sneak-attack in melee.

## Attacks of opportunity (AoO)

Provoked by certain actions in a threatened square (5 ft. of an opponent who can attack): casting a spell, drinking a potion, retrieving a stored item, standing from prone, moving out of a threatened square (unless 5-ft. step). One AoO per round per combatant (Combat Reflexes adds more, up to Dex mod).

## Movement in combat

- **Move action**: walk up to your speed (30 ft. for most).
- **Double move**: full-round; cover up to 2× speed.
- **Run**: full-round; 4× speed (×3 in heavy armor); straight line only.
- **5-foot step**: free; once per round; cannot also take other movement.
- **Charge**: full-round; move up to 2× speed in straight line, attack at +2 to hit, -2 AC for the round. Must end adjacent to target.
- **Withdraw**: full-round; first square moved isn't a threatened-square AoO.

## Grappling

1. Touch attack to grab (provokes AoO unless Improved Grab).
2. Opposed grapple check: `1d20 + BAB + Str mod + size mod`. Win = grappled (both flat-footed against others).
3. Subsequent rounds: grapple checks for actions — pin, damage, escape, cast (Concentration), draw light weapon.
